Section 1:
25.1232.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Castaneda, Barta, Thomas, Weston
Representative D. Ruby
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the office of management and budget for a
2 grant; and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - 2023-25 BIENNIUM - OFFICE OF MANAGEMENT AND
5 BUDGET - STATE DISASTER RELIEF FUND - ONE-TIME FUNDING. There is appropriated
6 out of any moneys in the state disaster relief fund in the state treasury, not otherwise
7 appropriated, the sum of $36,318, or so much of the sum as may be necessary, to the office of
8 management and budget for the purpose of providing a grant to the private investigation and
9 security board for costs incurred as the result of an unlawful protest of a pipeline, for the period
10 beginning with the effective date of this Act, and ending June 30, 2025. The funding
11 appropriated in this section is considered a one-time funding item.
12 SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
