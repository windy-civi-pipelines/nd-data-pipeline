Section 1:
25.8072.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
State and Local Government Committee
(At the request of the State Treasurer)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subdivision xx of subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 and
2 section 54-11-16 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to criminal history record checks
3 by the state treasurer for individuals with access to federal tax information.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subdivision xx of subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 of the North
6 Dakota Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 xx. The office of state treasurer for each individualan applicant or employee who has
8 access to federal tax information under chapter 54 - 11 - 16 .
9 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Section 54-11-16 of the North Dakota Century Code is
10 amended and reenacted as follows:
11 54-11-16. Criminal history record checks.
12 The state treasurer shall require any applicant, or employee, or independent contractor who
13 has access to federal tax information to submit to a statewide and nationwide criminal history
14 record check, and subsequent recheck within tenfive years from the date of the previous
15 criminal history record check. The nationwide criminal history record check must be conducted
16 in the manner provided by section 12-60-24.
