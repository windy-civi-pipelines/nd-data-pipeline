Section 1:
25.0879.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Longmuir, Beltz, Dockter, Nelson, Richter
Senators Bekkedahl, Klein, Patten
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 20.1-02 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to educational and community outreach programs established by the
3 game and fish department.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 20.1-02 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Educational and community outreach programs.
8 The department may establish educational and community outreach programs to promote
9 public understanding of fisheries and wildlife management and promote interest in pursuing
10 careers in fields employed by the department. The director may establish programs and
11 determine participation eligibility, including the direct participation in job shadowing of field
12 activities and ride alongs.
