Section 1:
25.0099.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Pyle, Frelich, Grueneich, Novak
Senators Kessel, Klein, Myrdal
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 57-39.2-04 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to a sales tax exemption for fire departments; and to provide an
3 effective date.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new subsection to section 57-39.2-04 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 created and enacted as follows:
7 Gross receipts from sales when made to a fire department for the purpose of providing
8 fire protection services. For purposes of this subsection, "fire department" means a fire
9 department or fire district that has filed a certificate of existence under section
10 18 - 04 - 02 which has been approved .
11 SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. This Act is effective for taxable events occurring after
12 June 30, 2025.
