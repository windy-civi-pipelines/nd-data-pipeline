25.0186.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Conference Committee Amendments
Introduced by
Appropriations Committee
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation for defraying the expenses of the department of
2 career and technical education; to provide an exemption; and to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION. The funds provided in this section, or so much of the funds
5 as may be necessary, are appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state
6 treasury, not otherwise appropriated, and from other funds derived from special funds and
7 federal funds, to the department of career and technical education for the purpose of defraying
8 the expenses of the department of career and technical education, for the biennium beginning
9 July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027, as follows:
10 Adjustments or
11 Base Level Enhancements Appropriation
12 Salaries and wages $5,118,145 $720,245 $5,838,390
13 New and vacant FTE pool 0 126,203 126,203
14 Operating expenses 3,046,350 1,699,261 4,745,611
15 Grants 11,507,349 2,500,000 14,007,349
16 Grants - secondary 41,537,780 10,500,000 52,037,780
17 Marketplace for kids 300,000 100,000 400,000
18 Science, technology, engineering, and 100,000 0 100,000
19 mathematics initiative
20 Adult farm management 1,706,138 (1,706,138) 0
21 Workforce training 2,500,000 750,000 3,250,000
22 Total all funds $65,815,762 $14,689,571 $80,505,333

4 SECTION 2. ONE-TIME FUNDING - EFFECT ON BASE BUDGET - REPORT TO
5 SEVENTIETH LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY. The following amounts reflect the one-time funding
6 items included in the appropriation in section 1 of this Act which are not included in the entity's
7 base budget for the 2027-29 biennium and which the entity shall report to the appropriations
8 committees of the seventieth legislative assembly regarding the use of this funding:
9 One-Time Funding Description General Fund Other Funds Total
10 Virtual reality career exploration $0 $1,000,000 $1,000,000
11 Rent, moving, and office equipment 300,000 0 300,000
12 expenses
13 Total $300,000 $1,000,000 $1,300,000
14 SECTION 3. NEW AND VACANT FTE POOL - LIMITATION - TRANSFER REQUEST. The
15 department of career and technical education may not spend funds appropriated in the new and
16 vacant FTE pool line item in section 1 of this Act, but may request the office of management and
17 budget to transfer funds from the new and vacant FTE pool line item to the salaries and wages
18 line item in accordance with the guidelines and reporting provisions included in House Bill
19 No. 1015, as approved by the sixty-ninth legislative assembly.
20 SECTION 4. OTHER FUNDS - STRATEGIC INVESTMENT AND IMPROVEMENTS FUND.
21 The other funds line item in section 1 of this Act includes the sum of $1,000,000 from the
22 strategic investment and improvements fund for the purpose of purchasing virtual reality career
23 exploration hardware and software.
24 SECTION 5. EXEMPTION - STATEWIDE AREA CAREER CENTER INITIATIVE GRANT
25 PROGRAM. The sum of $74,595,701 appropriated from the federal coronavirus capital projects
26 fund for the statewide area career center initiative grant program, of which $68,276,228 was
27 appropriated in section 1 of chapter 548 of the 2021 Special Session Session Laws and
28 continued into the 2023-25 biennium in section 3 of chapter 27 of the 2023 Session Laws and
29 $6,319,473 was transferred in section 10 of chapter 21 of the 2023 Session Laws, is not subject
30 to section 54-44.1-11 and any unexpended funds from these appropriations may be continued
31 into the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027.
