Section 1:
25.1041.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Vetter, Christianson, Grueneich, Headland, D. Ruby, Holle, Toman, D.
Johnston, Heinert
Senators Cory, Castaneda
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 4 of section 39-21-39 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to motor vehicle window tint.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 4 of section 39-21-39 of the North Dakota Century
5 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 4. An individual may not operate a motor vehicle with any object, material, or tinting
7 displayed, affixed, or applied on the front windshield or any window unless the object,
8 material, or tinting in conjunction with the windshield upon which it is displayed,
9 affixed, or applied has a light transmittance of at least seventy percent or the object,
10 material, or tinting in conjunction with a window other than the windshield upon which
11 it is displayed, affixed, or applied has a light transmittance of at least fifty thirty - five
12 percent. This subsection does not apply to windows behind the operator if the motor
13 vehicle is equipped with outside mirrors on both sides thatwhich meet the
14 requirements of section 39-21-38.
