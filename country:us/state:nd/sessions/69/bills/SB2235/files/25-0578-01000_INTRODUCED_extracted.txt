25.0578.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Braunberger, Barta, Cory
Representatives Foss, Schneider
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 47-16-20 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to rents and the priority of payments under a rental agreement.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 47-16-20 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 47-16-20. Rents - When payable.
7 1. When there is no contract or usage to the contrary, the rent of agricultural and wild
8 land shall beis payable yearly at the end of each year. Rents of lodgings shall beare
9 payable monthly at the end of each month. Other rents shall beare payable quarterly
10 at the end of each quarter from the time the lease takes effect. The rent for a lease
11 shorter than the periods herein specified shall bein this subsection is payable atwhen
12 the termination of the lease is terminated.
13 2. If a rental payment by a lessee for a lodging under subsection 1 is insufficient to pay
14 the outstanding rental amount due and any late fees or other charges applied by the
15 lessor, the payment first must be applied to the outstanding rental amount due unless
16 otherwise specified by the lessee at the time of payment.
