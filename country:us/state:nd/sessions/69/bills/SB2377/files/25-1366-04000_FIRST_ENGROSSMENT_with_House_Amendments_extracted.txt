Section 1:
25.1366.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with House Amendments
Introduced by
Senators Boschee, Bekkedahl, Dwyer
Representatives Dockter, Koppelman, Louser
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 26.1-47-02.1 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to preferred provider arrangements and restrictions on dental care services.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 26.1-47-02.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 26.1-47-02.1. Fees for dental services - Prohibition.
7 1. As used in this section, "covered services" means dental care services for which a
8 reimbursement is available under an enrollee's plan or for which a reimbursement
9 would be available but for the application of a deductible, copayment, coinsurance,
10 waiting period, annual or lifetime maximum, or frequency limitation.
11 2. Except for fees for covered services, a preferred provider arrangement for a dental
12 plan may not directly or indirectly set or otherwise regulate the fees charged by the
13 preferred provider for dental care services.
14 3. A preferred provider arrangement may not restrict a covered person from receiving or
15 paying for additional dental care services that were denied by the covered person's
16 dental plan .
17 4. Unless disclosed to the covered person before receiving dental care, a covered
18 person receiving or paying for additional dental care services described in
19 subsection 3 may not be charged a rate in excess of the preferred provider
20 arrangement's contracted rate for covered services.
