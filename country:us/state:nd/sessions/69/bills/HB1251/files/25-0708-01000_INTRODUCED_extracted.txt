Section 1:
25.0708.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Osowski, Hauck, Heilman, Maki, Morton, Nehring, Schauer, Marschall
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 15.1-06 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the prohibition of school extracurricular activities on certain holidays.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 15.1-06 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Family days - Holidays - Extracurricular activities prohibited.
7 A school may not hold an extracurricular activity, including a practice, game, performance,
8 competition, meeting, or event, whether required or optional, on a family day. For purposes of
9 this section, a "family day" means New Year's Day, Memorial Day, the anniversary of the
10 Declaration of Independence, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day, as those terms are
11 defined under section 1 - 03 - 01, and Easter Sunday .
