Section 1:
25.0778.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Roers, Axtman, Gerhardt
Representatives Dobervich, Heinert, Novak
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 3 of section 15.1-19-16 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to student self-administration of asthma or anaphylaxis medication.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 3 of section 15.1-19-16 of the North Dakota
5 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 3. For purposes of this section, "emergency medication" includes a prescription drug
7 delivered by inhalation to alleviate asthmatic symptoms and ana device that contains a
8 premeasured dose of epinephrine autoinjectable pento prevent or treat a
9 life - threatening allergic reaction .
