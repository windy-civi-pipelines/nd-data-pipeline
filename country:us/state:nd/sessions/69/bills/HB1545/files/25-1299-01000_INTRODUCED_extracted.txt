Section 1:
25.1299.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Finley-DeVille, Brown, Davis, Dobervich, Holle
Senators Marcellais, Mathern, Walen
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 1-03-13 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to changing the name of First Nations Day to Indigenous Peoples Day.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 1-03-13 of the North Dakota Century Code is amended
5 and reenacted as follows:
6 1-03-13. First NationsIndigenous Peoples Day.
7 To recognize the indigenous peoples of the State of North Dakota and their contributions to
8 the state and to the United States, the governor shall issue a proclamation each year
9 designating the Friday before the second Monday in October as First NationsIndigenous
10 Peoples Day.
