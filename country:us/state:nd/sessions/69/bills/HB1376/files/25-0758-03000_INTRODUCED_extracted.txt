Section 1:
25.0758.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Bosch, Heinert, Porter
Senators Axtman, Davison
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 15.1-19 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to cardiopulmonary resuscitation training for high school students.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 15.1-19 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Cardiopulmonary resuscitation training - High school students .
7 1. Each high school shall offer students annual cardiopulmonary resuscitation training
8 with optional certification for a student who wishes to become certified, which must:
9 a. Be provided by an instructor certified by the American heart association, the
10 American red cross, or another nonprofit organization nationally recognized for
11 cardiopulmonary resuscitation training.
12 b. Be conducted according to guidelines established by a certifying organization
13 under subdivision a .
14 c. Include training to stop the bleeding of a severely injured person.
15 2. The superintendent of public instruction shall reimburse school districts for the costs
16 incurred to provide cardiopulmonary resuscitation certification to high school students.
