Section 1:
25.0638.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Roers, Larson, Rummel
Representatives Lefor, Nathe, Rohr
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 15-10-38.1 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to eligibility for the skilled workforce student loan repayment
3 program; and to amend and reenact subsection 4 of section 15-10-38.2 of the North Dakota
4 Century Code, relating to eligibility for the skilled workforce scholarship program.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. A new subsection to section 15-10-38.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is
7 created and enacted as follows:
8 The requirement under subdivision c of subsection 4 is met if an individual completed
9 a master of science degree in nursing while teaching four clinical credits per year and
10 resides and works in this state in an occupation requiring the degree.
11 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Subsection 4 of section 15-10-38.2 of the North Dakota
12 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
13 4. The state board of higher education and the workforce development council shall
14 compile a list of qualifying educational programs annually. A qualifying educational
15 program is:
16 a. Is a program resulting in attainment of an associate's degree or lower credential
17 program that may be completed within four semesters, six quarters, or the final
18 two years of a baccalaureate program. A qualifying educational program also
19 must
20 b. Must pertain to the professional and technical skills and emerging occupations in
21 high demand in this state, as determined under subsection 3. Qualifying
22 educational programs may
23 c. May include degree or certificate programs.

Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 d. Notwithstanding any other requirements under this subsection, is a master of
2 science degree in nursing, if the individual teaches four clinical credits per
3 calendar year.
