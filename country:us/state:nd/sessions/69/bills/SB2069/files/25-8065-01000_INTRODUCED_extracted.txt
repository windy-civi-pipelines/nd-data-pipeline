Section 1:
25.8065.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
State and Local Government Committee
(At the request of the Public Service Commission)
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 46-05 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to publication requirements.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 46-05 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Government publication.
7 A unit of government shall file a published notice with the secretary of state whenever the
8 unit of government is required by law to publish notice in a newspaper. Unless otherwise
9 provided by law, a notice required by law which is filed and posted by the secretary of state
10 fulfills publication requirements if a newspaper fails to publish the notice required by law.
