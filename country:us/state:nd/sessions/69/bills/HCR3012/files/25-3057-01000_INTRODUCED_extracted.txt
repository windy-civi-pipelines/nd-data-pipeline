Section 1:
25.3057.01000
Sixty-ninth
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Hagert, Dockter, Koppelman, Brandenburg
Senators Rummel, Wanzek
1 A concurrent resolution to repeal section 10 of article X of the Constitution of North Dakota,
2 relating to the statewide property tax levy of one mill for support of the state medical center at
3 the University of North Dakota; and to provide an effective date.
4 STATEMENT OF INTENT
5 This measure would eliminate the constitutional statewide property tax levy of one mill for
6 support of the state medical center at the University of North Dakota.
7 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF NORTH DAKOTA, THE
8 SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN:
9 That the following proposed repeal of section 10 of article X of the Constitution of North
10 Dakota is agreed to and must be submitted to the qualified electors of North Dakota at the
11 general election to be held in 2026, in accordance with section 16 of article IV of the
12 Constitution of North Dakota.
13 SECTION 1. REPEAL. Section 10 of article X of the Constitution of North Dakota is
14 repealed.
15 SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. If approved by the electors, this measure is effective for
16 taxable years beginning after December 31, 2026.
