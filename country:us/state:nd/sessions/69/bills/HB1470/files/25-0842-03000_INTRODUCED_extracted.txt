1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 20.1-03-12 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to hunting and fishing fees.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 20.1-03-12 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 20.1-03-12. Schedule of fees for licenses and permits.
7 The fees collected under this section for licenses and permits must be deposited with the
8 state treasurer and credited to the game and fish fund, except aquatic nuisance species fees
9 must be deposited with the state treasurer and credited to the aquatic nuisance species
10 program fund. Forty-five dollars of each nonresident big game hunting license fee must be used
11 for the private land initiative. The various license and permit fees are as follows:
12 1. For a resident, age sixteen and over, small game hunting license, tentwenty dollars.
13 2. For a nonresident small game hunting license, one hundredone hundred twenty
14 dollars.
15 3. For a resident big game hunting license, thirty dollars, except the fee for a licensee
16 under age sixteen is ten dollars, except as provided in a gubernatorial proclamation
17 issued pursuant to section 20.1-08-04.1.
18 4. Except for a nonresident who participates on the same basis as a resident in a lottery
19 for deer licenses remaining after the second lottery for residents under subsection 4 of
20 section 20.1-03-11, for a nonresident big game hunting license, two hundred fiftythree
21 hundred fifty dollars, and for a nonresident bow license, two hundred fiftythree
22 hundred fifty dollars, and a nonrefundable five dollar application fee must accompany
23 any lottery license fee under this subsection, except as provided in a gubernatorial
24 proclamation issued pursuant to section 20.1-08-04.1. For a nonresident who
