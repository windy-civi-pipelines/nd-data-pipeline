Section 1:
25.1173.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with House Amendments
Introduced by
Senators Braunberger, Schaible
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 15.1-01-04 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to the authority for the kindergarten through grade twelve
3 coordination council to enter contracts.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new subsection to section 15.1-01-04 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 created and enacted as follows:
7 The council may enter a contract to conduct a study to carry out an objective of this
8 section.
