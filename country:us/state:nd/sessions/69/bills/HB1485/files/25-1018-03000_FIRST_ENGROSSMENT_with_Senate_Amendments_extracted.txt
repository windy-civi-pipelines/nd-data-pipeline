Section 1:
25.1018.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Senate Amendments
Introduced by
Representatives McLeod, Rios, Satrom, Schauer, Swiontek, Weisz, Karls, Wagner
Senators Lee, Dever, Hogan, Cleary
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 50-24.1-02.6 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to the personal needs allowance amount for eligible beneficiaries.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 50-24.1-02.6 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 50-24.1-02.6. Medical assistance benefits - Eligibility criteria.
7 1. The department shall provide medical assistance benefits to otherwise eligible
8 persons who are medically needy persons who have countable income that does not
9 exceed an amount determined under subsection 2.
10 2. The department shall establish an income level for medically needy persons at an
11 amount no less than required by federal law.
12 3. The department shall provide medical assistance benefits to children and families
13 coverage groups and pregnant women without consideration of assets.
14 4. The department shall provide medical assistance benefits to otherwise eligible
15 pregnant women who are lawfully present in the United States.
16 5. The department may require, as a condition of eligibility, individuals eligible for
17 Medicare part A, B, or D to apply for such coverage.
18 6. The department shall provide a personal monthly needs allowance, to be reviewed
19 and adjusted annually based on inflation, in an amount not less than one hundred fifty
20 dollars.
