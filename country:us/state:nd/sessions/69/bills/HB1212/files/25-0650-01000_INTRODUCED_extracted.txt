Section 1:
25.0650.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Monson, K. Anderson, J. Olson, Pyle, M. Ruby, Tveit, Brandenburg
Senators Myrdal, Axtman, Barta, Dever
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to designating a portion of state highway 18 as the PFC. Henry Gurke
3 memorial highway; and to provide a continuing appropriation.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 PFC. Henry Gurke memorial highway - State highway 18 - Continuing appropriation .
8 The department shall designate state highway 18 from the intersection of state highway 5 to
9 the Canadian border as the PFC. Henry Gurke memorial highway and shall place signs along
10 the highway designating that name. The department may accept any appropriate signs or funds
11 donated to the department for placement of the signs. Any donated funds are appropriated to
12 the department on a continuing basis for the purpose of providing signs designating state
13 highway 18 as the PFC. Henry Gurke memorial highway .
