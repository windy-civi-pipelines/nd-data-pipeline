Section 1:
25.0134.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Porter, Bosch, Dockter, Heinert, Karls, Nathe, Schauer, Toman
Senators Axtman, Cleary, Roers, Schaible
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 3 of section 54-52-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the definition of correctional officer for purposes of the public
3 employees retirement system.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 3 of section 54-52-01 of the North Dakota Century
6 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 3. "Correctional officer" means a participating member who is employed as a correctional
8 officer by a political subdivision. The term includes an individual employed by a
9 correctional facility as defined in section 12-44.1-01, who is enrolled in, but has not yet
10 completed, a correctional officer course approved or certified by the North Dakota
11 department of corrections and rehabilitation.
