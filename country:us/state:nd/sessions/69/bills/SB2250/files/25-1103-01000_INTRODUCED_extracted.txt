Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by

Representatives Kasper, Klemin
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 32-03-30 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to damages for injuries to timber.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 32-03-30 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 32-03-30. Damages for wrongful injuries to timber.
7 1. For wrongfulwillful and wanton injuries to timber, trees, or underwood upon the land of
8 another, or removal thereof, the measure of damages is three times such a sum as
9 would compensate for the actual detriment, except when the trespass was casual and
10 involuntary or committed under the belief that the land belonged to the trespasser, or
11 when the wood was taken by the authority of highway officers for the purposes of a
12 highway. In such a case the.
13 2. For negligent injuries to timber, trees, or underwood upon the land of another, or
14 removal thereof, the measure of damages areis a sum equal to the actual detriment.
