Section 1:
25.1320.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Finley-DeVille, Brown, Davis, Ista
Senators Mathern, Walen
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the department of commerce for tribal tourism
2 grants; and to provide for a legislative management report.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE - TRIBAL TOURISM
5 GRANTS - ONE-TIME FUNDING - LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT REPORT. There is
6 appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state treasury, not otherwise
7 appropriated, the sum of $100,000, or so much of the sum as may be necessary, to the
8 department of commerce for the purpose of providing tribal tourism grants, for the biennium
9 beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. A tribal government in the state may apply to
10 the department of commerce for a grant of up to $20,000 to match funds committed by a tribal
11 government to promote and enhance tribal nation tourism opportunities. A grant application
12 must include a plan for using funds to promote and enhance tribal nation tourism opportunities
13 to be overseen by the department of commerce division of tourism. The department of
14 commerce shall report to legislative management regarding the distribution, use, and impact of
15 grants. The appropriation provided in this section is considered a one-time funding item.
