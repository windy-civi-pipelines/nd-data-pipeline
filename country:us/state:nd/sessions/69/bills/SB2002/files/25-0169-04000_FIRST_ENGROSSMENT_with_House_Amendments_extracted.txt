1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation for defraying the expenses of the judicial branch;
2 to create and enact a new subsection to section 54-06-08.2 of the North Dakota Century Code,
3 relating to credit and debit card fees; to amend and reenact sections 27-02-02 and 27-05-03 of
4 the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the salaries of justices of the supreme court and
5 salaries of district court judges; to provide for transfers; to provide for a report; and to provide an
6 exemption.

9 as may be necessary, are appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state

11 federal funds, to the judicial branch for the purpose of defraying the expenses of the judicial

1 b. Salaries and wages if actual salaries and wages savings from vacant positions
2 and employee turnover, adjusted for other uses, are less than the estimate used
3 by the sixty-ninth legislative assembly in the development of the appropriation.
4 2. The supreme court shall report to the budget section regarding the use of funding in
5 the pool, including information on:
6 a. New FTE positions, including the date hired;

1 27-02-02. Salaries of justices of supreme court.
2 The annual salary of each justice of the supreme court is one hundred seventy-nine
3 thousand three hundred twelve dollars through June 30, 2024, and one hundred eighty-six
4 thousand four hundred eighty-four dollars thereaftertwo hundred seven thousand two hundred
5 forty - nine dollars . The chief justice of the supreme court is entitled to receive an additional five
6 thousand seventy-one dollars per annum through June 30, 2024, and five thousand two
7 hundred seventy-four seven thousand six hundred forty - seven dollars per annum thereafter.
8 SECTION 7. AMENDMENT. Section 27-05-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is
9 amended and reenacted as follows:
10 27-05-03. Salaries and expenses of district judges.
11 The annual salary of each district judge is one hundred sixty-four thousand five hundred
12 thirty-two dollars through June 30, 2024, and one hundred seventy-one thousand one hundred
13 thirteen dollars thereafter one hundred eighty - four thousand three hundred sixty - six dollars .
14 Each district judge is entitled to travel expenses, including mileage and subsistence while
15 engaged in the discharge of official duties outside the city in which the judge's chambers are
16 located. The salary and expenses are payable monthly in the manner provided by law. A
17 presiding judge of a judicial district is entitled to receive an additional four thousand six hundred
18 seventy-five five thousand one hundred sixty - two dollars per annum through June 30, 2024, and
19 four thousand eight hundred sixty-two dollars thereafter.
20 SECTION 8. A new subsection to section 54-06-08.2 of the North Dakota Century Code is
21 created and enacted as follows:
22 The judicial branch may charge a reasonable fee not exceeding the discount charged
23 by the credit or debit card issuer. The fee may be added to a payment as a service
24 charge for the acceptance of a payment made by a credit or debit card. The state
25 court administrator shall determine which nationally recognized cards will be accepted
26 for payments made to the judicial branch under this section.
27 SECTION 9. EXEMPTION - TRANSFERS. Notwithstanding section 54-16-04, the director
28 of the office of management and budget shall transfer appropriation authority between line items
29 in section 1 of this Act, as requested by the supreme court upon a finding by the court that the
30 nature of the duties of the court and its staff requires the transfers to carry on properly the
31 functions of the judicial branch of government.
