Section 1:
25.0863.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Heinert, Hauck, Jonas, Marschall, Meier, Novak, M. Ruby
Senator Cory
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 3 of section 15-19-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the payment of fees for courses provided by the center for distance
3 education.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 3 of section 15-19-01 of the North Dakota Century
6 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 3. A center for distance education student's school district of residence shall paymay
8 charge fees for a course, as may be prescribed by the superintendent of public
9 instruction, which must be paid by the student's:
10 a. Parent or legal guardian; or
11 b. School district of residence, if the school does not offer the course:
12 (1) That semester; or
13 (2) At a time the student has availability in the student's preferred class
14 schedule.
