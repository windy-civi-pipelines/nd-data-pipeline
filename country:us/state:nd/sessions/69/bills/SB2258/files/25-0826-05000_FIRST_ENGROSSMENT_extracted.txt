Section 1:
25.0826.05000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Senators Beard, Klein, Paulson
Representative Koppelman
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 28-32 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the enforcement of agency rules.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 28-32 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Agency enforcement of rules - Disclosure of authority .
7 When enforcing a rule adopted under this chapter, including through an official notice,
8 determination, order, or similar action, an agency shall specifically reference the statute or rule
9 providing the authority upon which the agency's action is based within ten days of receiving a
10 request for the information from the person against which the rule is being enforced.
