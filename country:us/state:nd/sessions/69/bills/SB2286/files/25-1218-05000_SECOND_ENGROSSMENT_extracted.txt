Section 1:
25.1218.05000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
SECOND ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Senators Erbele, Mathern
Representatives Dobervich, Nelson
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a legislative management study regarding university of North
2 Dakota health profession programs.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - UNIVERSITY OF NORTH DAKOTA
5 HEALTH PROFESSIONS COLLABORATIVE FACILITY. During the 2025-26 interim, the
6 legislative management shall consider studying the funding necessary to provide education and
7 internships statewide for all programs at an expanded university of North Dakota health
8 professions collaborative facility. The study may consider input from the university of North
9 Dakota school of medicine and health sciences advisory council and other statutory boards and
10 councils related to all health profession degrees provided by the university of North Dakota in
11 the development of the health professions collaborative facility. The legislative management
12 shall report its findings and recommendations, together with any legislation necessary to
13 implement the recommendations to the seventieth legislative assembly.
