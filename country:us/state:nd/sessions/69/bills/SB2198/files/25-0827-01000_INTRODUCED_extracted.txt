1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 37-01-25 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to national guard or federal service leave of absence for state and political subdivision
3 employees.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 37-01-25 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 amended and reenacted as follows:
7 37-01-25. Officers and employees of state or political subdivisions in national guard
8 or federal service to retain status for period of active service or any military duty.
9 All officers and employees of this state or of a political subdivision of this state who:
10 1. Are membersFor purposes of this section, "officer or employee" means:
11 a. A member of the national guard;
12 2. b. Are membersA member of the armed forces reserve of the United States of
13 America;
14 3. c. Shall beAn individual subject to call in the federal service by the president of the
15 United States; or
16 4. d. Shall volunteerAn individual who volunteers for such federal service,.
17 2. An officer or employee of the state or a political subdivision, when ordered by proper
18 authority to active noncivilian employment for any military duty, areis entitled to a leave
19 of absence from suchthe civil service for the period of suchthe active service or
20 military duty including traveling to and from a duty station without loss of status or
21 efficiency rating.
22 3. If such persons havethe individual has been in the continuous employ of the state or
23 political subdivision for ninety days immediately preceding the leave of absence, they

2 loss of pay. In addition, any
3 4. A leave of absence necessitated by a full or partial mobilization of the reserve and
