Section 1:
25.0970.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Senate Amendments
Introduced by
Representatives Steiner, Bahl, McLeod, Pyle, Schauer, Toman, Kasper
Senators Cleary, Sickler, Weston
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subdivision b of subsection 1 of section 28-32-10 of the
2 North Dakota Century Code, relating to the required abbreviated notice for administrative
3 rulemaking.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subdivision b of subsection 1 of section 28-32-10 of the North
6 Dakota Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 b. The agency or commission shall request publication of an abbreviated
8 newspaper publication notice at least once in each official county newspaper
9 published in this state. The abbreviated newspaper publication of notice, which
10 must be:
11 (1) Be in a display-type format with a minimum width of one column of
12 approximately two inches [5.08 centimeters] and a minimum depth of
13 approximately three inches [7.62 centimeters] and with a headline
14 describing the general topic of the proposed rules. The notice must also
15 include
16 (2) Include the telephone number or address to use to obtain a copy of the
17 proposed rules, identification of the emergency status and declared effective
18 date of any emergency rules, the address to use and the deadline to submit
19 written comments, and the location, date, and time of the public hearing on
20 the rules , and a short description of the general subject matter of the
21 proposed rules submission. The notice also may include a quick response

1 code providing access to the full notice or additional information related to
2 the proposed rules at the discretion of the agency or commission.
