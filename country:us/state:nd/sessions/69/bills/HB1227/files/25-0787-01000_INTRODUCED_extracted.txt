Section 1:
25.0787.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Toman, Dockter, D. Johnston, Koppelman, Porter
Senators Cory, Paulson, Schaible
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 1-08 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to the adoption of central standard time; and to repeal section 40-01-20 of the
3 North Dakota Century Code, relating to daylight saving time.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 1-08 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Standard time.
8 1. Under 15 U.S.C. 260(a), the state is exempt from the establishment of daylight saving
9 time.
10 2. Greenwich mean time minus five hours is the official standard time of the portions of
11 the state in the central time zone.
12 3. Greenwich mean time minus six hours is the official standard time of the portions of
13 the state in the mountain time zone.
14 4. This section may not affect the standard time established by United States law
15 governing the movements of common carriers engaged in interstate commerce or the
16 time for performance of an act by an officer or department of the United States, as
17 established by a statute, lawful order, rule, or regulation of the United States or an
18 agency of the United States.
19 SECTION 2. REPEAL. Section 40-01-20 of the North Dakota Century Code is repealed.
