Section 1:
25.0513.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Senators Wobbema, Luick, Paulson, Weston
Representatives D. Johnston, J. Olson
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 20.1-03-11 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to disabled veteran deer hunting licenses.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new subsection to section 20.1-03-11 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 created and enacted as follows:
6 A resident disabled veteran who has a one hundred percent service-connected
7 disability as determined by the department of veterans' affairs shall receive one bonus
8 point each year to an application for a deer license issued by lottery. Bonus points
9 received under this section are in addition to any other bonus points received by the
10 department and must reset after receiving a deer license issued by lottery.
