Section 1:
25.0681.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives D. Ruby, Fegley, Grueneich, Maki, Motschenbacher, Osowski
Senators Castaneda, Larson
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact two new subsections to section 39-06.1-11 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to temporary restricted licenses; and to provide a penalty.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. Two new subsections to section 39-06.1-11 of the North Dakota Century Code
5 are created and enacted as follows:
6 An individual who has a temporary restricted driver's license and commits a moving
7 traffic violation is subject to the fee specified for the violation, plus an additional fee of
8 fifty dollars.
9
10 The director shall revoke an individual's temporary restricted license if the individual
11 commits three moving traffic violations after being issued the temporary restricted
12 license. A revocation under this subsection may only last a period of thirty days.
