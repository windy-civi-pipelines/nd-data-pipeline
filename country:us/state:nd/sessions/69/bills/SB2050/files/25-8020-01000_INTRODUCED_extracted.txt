Section 1:
25.8020.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
State and Local Government Committee
(At the request of the Attorney General)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 1 of section 54-12-24 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the director of the state crime laboratory.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 1 of section 54-12-24 of the North Dakota Century
5 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 1. A state crime laboratory is created as a division of the office of the attorney general.
7 This division consists of a director, the state toxicologist, and such other personnel as
8 may be appointed by the attorney general. The state crime laboratory may establish
9 and charge fees for services rendered. The state crime laboratory must be
10 administratively separated from the bureau of criminal investigation. The director
11 serves at the pleasure of the attorney general and is entitled to receive a salary set by
12 the attorney general within the limits of legislative appropriation.
