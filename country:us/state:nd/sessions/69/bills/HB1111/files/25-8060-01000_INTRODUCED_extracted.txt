Section 1:
25.8060.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Energy and Natural Resources Committee
(At the request of the Public Service Commission)
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 49-02 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to public interest exemptions for electric public utilities.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 49-02 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Public interest exemption - Rate regulation.
7 The commission may exempt an electric public utility from a regulation that affects rates,
8 contracts, services rendered, adequacy, or sufficiency of facilities, or the rules or restrictions of
9 a public utility if the commission finds an exemption in the public interest. The commission may
10 apply terms and conditions to an exemption under this section as the commission deems
11 reasonable and proper. This section does not limit the authority of the commission granted
12 under chapter 49 - 03. An exemption under this section may be revoked or suspended in the
13 manner prescribed by chapter 28 - 32 .
