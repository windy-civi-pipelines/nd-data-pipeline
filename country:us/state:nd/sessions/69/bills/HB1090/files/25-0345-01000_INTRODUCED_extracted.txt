Section 1:
25.0345.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives K. Anderson, Beltz, Hagert, D. Johnston, Monson, Osowski, D. Ruby, Vetter
Senators Clemens, Myrdal, Weston
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to designating the bridge on state highway 18 as the Specialist 4
3 Richard W. Orsund Vietnam bridge; to provide a continuing appropriation; and to declare an
4 emergency.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
7 and enacted as follows:
8 Specialist 4 Richard W. Orsund Vietnam bridge - State highway 18 - Continuing
9 appropriation.
10 The department shall designate the bridge on state highway 18 between the junction o f
11 sixty-ninth street northeast and the junction of seventieth street northeast as the Specialis t 4
12 Richard W. Orsund Vietnam bridge and shall place signs along the highway designating tha t
13 name. The department may accept any appropriate signs or funds donated to the department
14 for the placement of signs. Any donated funds are appropriated to the department on a
15 continuing basis for the purpose of providing signs designating the bridge on state highway 18
16 as the Specialis t 4 Richard W. Orsund Vietnam bridge .
17 SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
