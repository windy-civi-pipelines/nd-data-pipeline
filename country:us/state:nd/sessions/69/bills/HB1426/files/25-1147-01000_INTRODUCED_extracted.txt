Section 1:
25.1147.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Dockter, Holle, Martinson, Porter, Rohr, Toman, Heinert
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to designating the bridge on state highway 6 as the CPL Raymond
3 Porter Korea bridge; and to provide a continuing appropriation.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 CPL Raymond Porter Korea bridge - State highway - Continuing appropriation.
8 The department shall designate the bridge on state highway 6 between the junction o f
9 county road one thirty - seven and the junction of fifty - third street as the CPL Raymond Porter
10 Korea bridge and shall place signs along the avenue designating that name. The department
11 may accept any appropriate signs or funds donated to the department for the placement of
12 signs. Any donated funds are appropriated to the department on a continuing basis for the
13 purpose of providing signs designating the bridge on state highway 6 as the CPL Raymond
14 Porter Korea bridge.
