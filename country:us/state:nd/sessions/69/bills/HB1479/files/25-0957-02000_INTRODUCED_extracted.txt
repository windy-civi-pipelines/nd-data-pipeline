Section 1:
25.0957.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Karls, Berg, Bosch, Dockter, Hauck, Martinson, Nathe
Senators Dever, Gerhardt, Larson
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 1-03 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to the designation and establishment of North Dakota Constitution Day.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 North Dakota Constitution Day.
7 To commemorate the adoption of the Constitution of North Dakota on Augus t 17, 1889, by
8 constitutional convention, and the ratification by vote on October 1, 1889, by the people of the
9 territory that would become North Dakota, and to encourage state citizens to seek greater
10 knowledge and understanding of the Constitution of North Dakota, the first of October of each
11 year is designated and established as North Dakota Constitution Day.
