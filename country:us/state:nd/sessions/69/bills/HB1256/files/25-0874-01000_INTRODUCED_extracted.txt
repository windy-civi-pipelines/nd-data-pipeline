Section 1:
25.0874.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Grueneich, Brandenburg, Dockter, Jonas, Ostlie, Satrom
Senators Conley, Erbele
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the parks and recreation department for a
2 walking trail recreation grant.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - PARKS AND RECREATION DEPARTMENT - WALKING
5 TRAIL RECREATION GRANT - ONE-TIME FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any
6 moneys in the general fund in the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of
7 $75,000, or so much of the sum as may be necessary, to the parks and recreation department
8 for the purpose of providing a grant to a rural community experiencing a significant population
9 increase for a walking trail expansion project, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and
10 ending June 30, 2027. This funding is considered a one-time funding item.
