Section 1:
25.1080.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Headland, D. Anderson, Koppelman, Vollmer, J. Olson, Hagert
Senators Kessel, Meyer, Rummel, Thomas
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 4 of section 57-51.1-03 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the oil extraction tax rate reduction for oil produced from a new well
3 drilled and completed outside the Bakken and Three Forks formations; and to provide an
4 effective date.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 4 of section 57-51.1-03 of the North Dakota
7 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
8 4. The first seventy-five thousand one hundred twenty -five thousand barrels of oil
9 produced during the first eighteen thirty - six months after completion, from a well drilled
10 and completed outside the Bakken and Three Forks formations, and ten miles [16.10
11 kilometers] or more outside an established field in which the industrial commission has
12 defined the pool to include the Bakken or Three Forks formation, is subject to a
13 reduced tax rate of two percent of the gross value at the well of the oil extracted under
14 this chapter.
15 SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. This Act is effective for taxable production beginning after
16 June 30, 2025.
