Section 1:
25.8028.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Judiciary Committee
(At the request of the Supreme Court)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 14-09.1-07 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to mediation agreements.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 14-09.1-07 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 14-09.1-07. Mediation agreementsummary.
7 The mediator shall reduce to writingprepare a written summary of any agreement of the
8 parties. The mediator shall inform the parties of their right to review the agreement with counsel
9 before they sign the agreement. After the agreement issummary may not be shown to the court
10 unless signed by the parties, the mediator shall present the agreement to the court. The
11 agreement is not binding upon the parties until approved by order of the court. The mediator
12 shall advise the court whether an agreement was reached.
