Section 1:
25.0493.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Schaible, Lemm, Myrdal
Representatives Brandenburg, Hauck, Holle
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 20.1-01-21 of the North Dakota Century Code;
2 relating to hunting near buildings, occupied structures, or storage structures.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 20.1-01-21 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 20.1-01-21. Hunting near occupied buildinga building, occupied structure, or storage
7 structure without permission unlawful.
8 No personAn individual may not hunt or pursue game upon the premises of another, within
9 four hundred forty yards [402.34 meters] of any occupied building,a building, occupied structure,
10 or storage structure without consent of the person occupying such buildingthe consent of the
11 property owner.
