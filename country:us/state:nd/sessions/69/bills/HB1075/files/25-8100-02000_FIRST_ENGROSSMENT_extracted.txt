Section 1:
25.8100.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Government and Veterans Affairs Committee
(At the request of the Adjutant General)
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subdivision to subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 and
2 a new section to chapter 37-17.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to criminal history
3 record checks by the department of emergency services.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new subdivision to subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 of the North Dakota
6 Century Code is created and enacted as follows:
7 The department of emergency services for a final employment applicant under
8 chapter 37 - 17.1 .
9 SECTION 2. A new section to chapter 37-17.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
10 and enacted as follows:
11 Criminal history record checks.
12 The department of emergency services may require a final applicant for employment to
13 submit to statewide and nationwide criminal history record checks. The nationwide crimina l
14 history record check must be conducted in the manner provided by section 12 - 60 - 24 .
