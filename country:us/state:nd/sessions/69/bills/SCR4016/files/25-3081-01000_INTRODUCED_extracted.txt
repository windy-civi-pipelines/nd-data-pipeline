Section 1:
25.3081.01000
Sixty-ninth
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senator Walen
Representative Rios
1 A concurrent resolution directing the Legislative Management to consider studying primary
2 election procedures applying to partisan races and whether the use of a separate ballot based
3 on political party would enrich the primary election held in the state.
4 WHEREAS, North Dakota is the only state in the United States not requiring voter
5 registration; and
6 WHEREAS, a qualified elector's recent voting history is an exempt record and is not
7 accessible for political purposes under Section 16.1-01-15; and
8 WHEREAS, political parties should have access to information relating to a qualified
9 elector's voting record in a primary election;
10 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF NORTH DAKOTA, THE
11 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING THEREIN:
12 That the Legislative Management consider studying primary election procedures applying to
13 partisan races and whether the use of a separate ballot based on political party would enrich
14 the primary election held in the state; and
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Legislative Management report its findings and
16 recommendations, together with any legislation required to implement the recommendations, to
17 the Seventieth Legislative Assembly.
