25.1098.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Murphy, Ista, Schreiber-Beck
Senators Barta, Cleary
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 2 of section 54-24.4-05 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to state aid to public libraries.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 2 of section 54-24.4-05 of the North Dakota
5 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 2. Approve the distribution of state funded grants to libraries, except for grants distributed
7 under chapter 54-24.2.
