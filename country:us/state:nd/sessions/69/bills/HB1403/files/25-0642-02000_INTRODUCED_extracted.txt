Section 1:
25.0642.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Schneider, Brandenburg, Grueneich, D. Johnston, Klemin, Pyle, Davis,
Hager
Senators Castaneda, Clemens, Dever, Marcellais
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 1-03 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to the designation of Prisoner of War and Missing in Action Day as a state
3 holiday.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Prisoner of War and Missing in Action Day.
8 The third Friday in September of each year is Prisoner of War and Missing in Action Day. To
9 remember and honor the sacrifices of those who were held captive and returned, as well as
10 those who remain missing, the governor shall issue a proclamation each year designating the
11 third Friday in September as Prisoner of War and Missing in Action Day, a state holiday. This
12 section may not be interpreted to make Prisoner of War and Missing in Action Day a day on
13 which schools and other entities are required to close.
