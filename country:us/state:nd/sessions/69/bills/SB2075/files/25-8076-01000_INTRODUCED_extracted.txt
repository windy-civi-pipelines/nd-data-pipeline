Section 1:
25.8076.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Transportation Committee
(At the request of the Highway Patrol)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 5 of section 39-32-02 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the exemption from the requirement to maintain a record of duty
3 status for certain intrastate drivers.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 5 of section 39-32-02 of the North Dakota Century
6 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 5. An intrastate driver is exempt from maintaining a record of duty status if:
8 a. The driver operates within a one hundred fifty air-mile radius from the driver's
9 normal work-reporting location or from the official worksite of the vehicle;
10 b. At least ten consecutive hours off duty separate each twelve hours on duty;
11 c. The driver, except for a driver salesperson, returns to the work-reporting location
12 and is released from work within twelvefourteen consecutive hours; and
13 d. The motor carrier maintains and retains for a period of six months accurate time
14 records showing the time the driver reports for duty and is released from duty
15 each day.
