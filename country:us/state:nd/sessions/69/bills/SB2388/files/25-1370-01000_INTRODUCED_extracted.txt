Section 1:
25.1370.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Van Oosting, Magrum, Boehm
Representatives VanWinkle, Morton
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 19-02.1 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the over-the-counter availability of ivermectin.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 19-02.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Ivermectin access.
7 1. As used in this section:
8 a. "Ivermectin" means a medication to treat parasitic infections approved by the
9 federal food and drug administration.
10 b. "Over-the-counter" means a medication legally sold and purchased without a
11 prescription.
12 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, ivermectin suitable for human use may be
13 sold and purchased as an over-the-counter medication in this state.
