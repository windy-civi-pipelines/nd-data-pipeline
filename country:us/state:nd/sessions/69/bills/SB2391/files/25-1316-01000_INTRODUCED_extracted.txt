Section 1:
25.1316.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senator Wanzek
Representatives Headland, Vigesaa
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to designating Stutsman county road 62 as part of the state highway
3 system.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Stutsman county road 62 - State highway system.
8 The director shall designate Stutsman county road 62 from the junction of state highway 46
9 to the junction of interstate highway 94 as a part of the state highway system .
