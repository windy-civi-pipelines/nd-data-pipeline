Section 1:
25.0401.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Legislative Management
(Workforce Committee)
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the agriculture commissioner, the attorney
2 general, the department of career and technical education, the department of health and human
3 services, and the department of transportation to increase the use of uncrewed aircraft systems,
4 autonomous vehicles, or other autonomous technologies in the state; to provide for a legislative
5 management study; and to provide for a legislative management report.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
7 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - AGRICULTURE COMMISSIONER - ONE-TIME
8 FUNDING - REPORT TO THE LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT.
9 1. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state treasury, not
10 otherwise appropriated, the sum of $750,000, or so much of the sum as may be
11 necessary, to the agriculture commissioner for the purpose of providing uncrewed
12 aircraft system, autonomous vehicle, or other autonomous technology grants to
13 individuals and entities in the agriculture industry, for the biennium beginning July 1,
14 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. The funding provided in this subsection is
15 considered a one-time funding item.
16 2. The agriculture commissioner shall develop an application process and guidelines for
17 the grants, including eligibility criteria, maximum grant awards, and matching
18 requirements. The agriculture commissioner may require an individual or entity to
19 provide one dollar of matching funds for every four dollars awarded by the state. To be
20 eligible for a grant, an individual or entity shall certify to the agriculture commissioner
21 that the operation of the uncrewed aircraft system, autonomous vehicle, or other
22 autonomous technology is related to the inspection, operation, maintenance, or
23 construction of agriculture property or infrastructure.

1 3. An individual or entity may use grant funding to contract for services related to
2 uncrewed aircraft systems, autonomous vehicles, or other autonomous technologies;
3 or for other eligible uses related to uncrewed aircraft system, autonomous vehicle, or
4 other autonomous technology operations as determined by the agriculture
5 commissioner.
6 4. During the 2025-26 interim, the agriculture commissioner shall provide at least one
7 report to the legislative management regarding the grants, including a list of grant
8 recipients, the amounts awarded, and a description of the use of the grant funding.
9 SECTION 2. APPROPRIATION - ATTORNEY GENERAL - ONE TIME FUNDING -
10 REPORT TO THE LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT.
11 1. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state treasury, not
12 otherwise appropriated, the sum of $750,000, or so much of the sum as may be
13 necessary, to the attorney general for the purpose of providing uncrewed aircraft
14 system, autonomous vehicle, or other autonomous technology grants to individuals
15 and entities aiding the bureau of criminal investigation in conducting missing person
16 searches, crime scene reconstruction, and law enforcement first response operations,
17 for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. The funding

19 2. The attorney general shall develop an application process and guidelines for the
20 grants, including eligibility criteria, maximum grant awards, and matching

26 enforcement first response operations.
27 3. An individual or entity may use grant funding to contract for services related to
28 uncrewed aircraft systems, autonomous vehicles, or other autonomous technologies;
29 or for other eligible uses related to uncrewed aircraft system, autonomous vehicle, or
30 other autonomous technology operations as determined by the attorney general.

1 must include an analysis of the sectors using autonomous systems and the sectors that could
2 benefit from the use of autonomous systems. The study also must quantify the return on

5 report its findings and recommendations, together with any legislation required to implement the
6 recommendations, to the seventieth legislative assembly.
