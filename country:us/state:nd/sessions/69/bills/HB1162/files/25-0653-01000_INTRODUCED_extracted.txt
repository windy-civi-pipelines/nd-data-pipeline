Sixty-ninth

Introduced by
Representatives Schauer, Jonas, Koppelman, McLeod, Pyle, Stemen, Swiontek, Warrey
Senators Lee, Sorvaag, Clemens
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 1 of section 61-39-03 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the board of directors of the Lake Agassiz water authority.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 1 of section 61-39-03 of the North Dakota Century
5 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 1. The authority must be governed by a board of directors selected as follows:
7 a. One member from a city with a population greater than forty thousand located
8 east of state highway 1 and north of state highway 200.
9 b. One memberTwo members from a citydiffering cities with a population greater
10 than forty thousand located east of state highway 1 and south of state
11 highway 200.
12 c. One member from a city with a population of five thousand but not more than
13 forty thousand located east of state highway 1.
14 d. One member from a city with a population of less than five thousand located east
15 of state highway 1.
16 e. Two members from water districts located east of state highway 1 and north of
17 state highway 200.
18 f. Two members from water districts located east of state highway 1 and south of
19 state highway 200.
20 g. One member from water districts located east of state highway 1.
21 h. One member from a Minnesota city with a population of more than thirty
22 thousand and which is located within five miles [8.05 kilometers] of this state.
23 i. One member from water districts located west of state highway 1.
24 j. One member from a city west of state highway 1.
