Section 1:
25.3100.01000
Sixty-ninth
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Conmy, Vetter, Hendrix, Christianson, Dockter, Dobervich
Senator Braunberger
1 A concurrent resolution directing the Legislative Management to consider studying the
2 regulatory framework and potential economic impact of tetrahydrocannabinol infused
3 beverages.
4 WHEREAS, the North Dakota Department of Agriculture regulates hemp and other varieties
5 of cannabis sativa for agriculture and industrial purposes; and
6 WHEREAS, the tetrahydrocannabinol in cannabis beverages often is derived from hemp
7 because it is legal to produce under federal law; and
8 WHEREAS, tetrahydrocannabinol and cannabinoid hemp beverages are emerging in the
9 national marketplace as an alternative to alcoholic beverage with fewer after effects;
10 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
11 NORTH DAKOTA, THE SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN:
12 That the Legislative Management consider studying regulatory framework and potential
13 economic impact of tetrahydrocannabinol infused beverages in the state; and
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Legislative Management report its findings and
15 recommendations, together with any legislation required to implement the recommendations, to
16 the Seventieth Legislative Assembly.
