Section 1:
25.1064.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Senate Amendments
Introduced by
Representatives Christy, Bahl, Berg, Conmy, Grindberg, Hoverson, Mitskog, J. Olson
Senators Boschee, Myrdal, Sickler, Thomas
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact sections 12.1-27.2-01 and 12.1-27.2-04.1 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to a prohibition on possessing certain computer-generated
3 images; and to provide a penalty.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 12.1-27.2-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 amended and reenacted as follows:
7 12.1-27.2-01. Definitions.
8 As used in this chapter:
9 1. "Computer-generated image" means an image or visual representation created
10 through the use of artificial intelligence or other computer program.
11 2. "Minor" means an individual under eighteen years of age or a computer-generated
12 image used, created, adapted, altered, or modified to appear to depict an individual
13 under eighteen years of age by face, likeness, or other distinguishing characteristics.
14 3. "Obscene sexual performance" means any performance which includes sexual
15 conduct by a minor in any obscene material or obscene performance, as defined in
16 section 12.1-27.1-01.
17 2.4. "Performance" means any play, motion picture, photograph, dance, or other visual
18 representation, or any part of a performance.
19 3.5. "Promote" means to procure, manufacture, issue, sell, give, provide, lend, mail,
20 deliver, transfer, transmit, ship, transport, publish, distribute, circulate, disseminate,
21 present, exhibit, or advertise.
22 4.6. "Sexual conduct" means actual or simulated:
Page No. 1 25.1064.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 a. Sexual intercourse;
2 b. Sodomy, as defined under section 12.1-27.1-01;
3 c. Sexual bestiality;
4 d. Masturbation;
5 e. Sadomasochistic abuse, as defined under section 12.1-27.1-01;
6 f. Lewd exhibition of the buttocks, breasts, or genitals;
7 g. Nude or partially denuded human figure, as defined in section 12.1-27.1-03.1, if
8 depicted for the purpose of the sexual stimulation or the sexual gratification of
9 any individual who may view such depiction; or
10 h. Physical contact with a person's clothed or unclothed genitals, pubic area,
11 buttocks, or breasts. It is not necessary that the minor know that he or she is
12 participating in the described conduct, or any aspect of it.
13 5.7. "Sexual performance" means any performance which includes sexual conduct by a
14 minor.
15 6.8. "Simulated" means the explicit depiction of any of the conduct set forth in subsection 4
16 which creates the appearance of actual sexual conduct and which exhibits any nude
17 or partially denuded human figure, as defined in section 12.1-27.1-03.1.
18 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Section 12.1-27.2-04.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is
19 amended and reenacted as follows:
20 12.1-27.2-04.1. Possession of certain materials prohibited.
21 1. A person is guilty of a class C felony if, knowing of its character and content, that
22 person knowingly possesses any motion picture, computer-generated image,
23 photograph, or other visual representation that includes sexual conduct by a minor.
24 2. An individual violating this section is guilty of a class B felony if :
25 a. The offense involved twenty or more images;
26 b. The offense involved sadistic or masochistic conduct or other depictions of
27 violence;
28 c. The offense involved sexual bestiality;
29 d. The offense involves a prepubescent minor or a minor under twelve years of age;
30 or
Page No. 2 25.1064.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 e. The individual has previously been convicted of an offense subject to registration
2 under section 12.1 - 32 - 15 .
3 3. This section does not impose liability on the provider of an interactive computer
4 service, as defined in 47 U.S.C. 230, or an internet service provider, cloud computer
5 provider, cybersecurity provider, communication service provider, or information
6 service or telecommunication service network, as defined in 47 U.S.C. 153, for any
7 content provided by another person.
