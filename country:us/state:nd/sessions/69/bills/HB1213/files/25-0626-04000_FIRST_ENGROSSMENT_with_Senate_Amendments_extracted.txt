Section 1:
25.0626.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Senate Amendments
Introduced by
Representatives Frelich, K. Anderson, Heinert, Mitskog, Schreiber-Beck, Stemen
Senators Myrdal, Barta, Luick
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a transfer to the infrastructure revolving loan fund.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
3 SECTION 1. TRANSFER - STRATEGIC INVESTMENT AND IMPROVEMENTS FUND -
4 INFRASTRUCTURE REVOLVING LOAN FUND. The office of management and budget shall
5 transfer $20,000,000 from the strategic investment and improvements fund to the infrastructure
6 revolving loan fund during the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027.
