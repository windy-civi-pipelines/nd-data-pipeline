Section 1:
25.0180.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with House Amendments
Introduced by
Appropriations Committee
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation for defraying the expenses of the commissioner of
2 university and school lands; to provide for distributions from permanent funds; and to provide for
3 a report.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION. The funds provided in this section, or so much of the funds
6 as may be necessary, are appropriated out of any moneys from special funds, to the
7 commissioner of university and school lands for the purpose of defraying the expenses of the
8 commissioner of university and school lands, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and
9 ending June 30, 2027, as follows:
10 Adjustments or
11 Base Level Enhancements Appropriation
12 Salaries and wages $8,005,331 $1,835,598 $9,840,929
13 New and vacant FTE pool 0 419,754 419,754
14 Operating expenses 1,763,694 (164,362) 1,599,332
15 Contingencies 100,000 0 100,000
16 Total special funds $9,869,025 $2,090,990 $11,960,015
17 Full-time equivalent positions 33.00 1.00 34.00
18 SECTION 2. NEW AND VACANT FTE POOL - LIMITATION - TRANSFER REQUEST. The
19 commissioner of university and school lands may not spend funds appropriated in the new and
20 vacant FTE pool line item in section 1 of this Act, but may request the office of management and
21 budget to transfer funds from the new and vacant FTE pool line item to the salaries and wages
Page No. 1 25.0180.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 line item in accordance with the guidelines and reporting provisions included in House Bill
2 No. 1015, as approved by the sixty-ninth legislative assembly.
3 SECTION 3. DISTRIBUTIONS TO STATE INSTITUTIONS. Pursuant to article IX of the
4 Constitution of North Dakota, the board of university and school lands shall distribute during the
5 biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027, the following amounts, from the
6 permanent funds managed for the benefit of the following entities:
7 Common schools $584,677,350
8 North Dakota state university 8,770,000
9 University of North Dakota 6,948,000
10 Youth correctional center 3,136,000
11 School for the deaf 2,388,000
12 North Dakota state college of science 2,570,284
13 State hospital 1,976,284
14 Veterans' home 994,284
15 Valley City state university 1,566,000
16 North Dakota vision services - school for the blind 1,936,284
17 Mayville state university 1,102,000
18 Dakota college at Bottineau 406,284
19 Dickinson state university 406,284
20 Minot state university 406,284
21 Total $617,283,338
22 SECTION 4. REVENUE DIVERSIFICATION AND DEVELOPMENT OPPORTUNITIES -
23 DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE - REPORT. During the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and
24 ending June 30, 2027, the commissioner of university and school lands and the commissioner
25 of commerce shall collaborate to identify revenue diversification and development opportunities
26 on trust lands. The commissioner of university and school lands shall report to the
27 appropriations committees of the seventieth legislative assembly on the results of the
28 collaboration to identify revenue diversification and development opportunities.
