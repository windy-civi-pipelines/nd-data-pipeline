Section 1:
25.0989.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senator Beard
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 52-08 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to workforce training center funding distribution.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 52-08 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Workforce training center funding distribution.
7 The state board for career and technical education shall distribute the total amount
8 appropriated for the biennium for workforce training as follows:
9 1. Sixty percent divided equally among the workforce training centers;
10 2. Twenty percent apportioned among the workforce training centers based on each
11 center's average number of training hours provided by each center during the first
12 three consecutive fiscal years of the previous four fiscal years immediately preceding
13 the year of distribution; and
14 3. Twenty percent apportioned among the workforce training centers based on each
15 center's average number of unduplicated participants reported by each center during
16 the first three consecutive fiscal years of the previous four fiscal years immediately
17 preceding the year of distribution.
