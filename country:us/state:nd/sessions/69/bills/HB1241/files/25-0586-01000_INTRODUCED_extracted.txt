25.0586.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Satrom, Ostlie
Senator Conley
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 39-21-26 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to special restrictions on lamps on motor vehicles or equipment.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 39-21-26 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 39-21-26. Special restrictions on lamps.
7 1. Any lighted lamp or illuminating device uponon a motor vehicle, other than headlamps,
8 spot lamps, auxiliary lamps, flashing turn signals, emergency vehicle warning lamps
9 and schoolbus warning lamps, which projects a beam of light of an intensity greater
10 than three hundred candlepower must be so directed so that no part of the
11 high-intensity portion of the beam will strike the level of the roadway on which the
12 vehicle stands at a distance of more than seventy-five feet [22.86 meters] from the
13 vehicle.
14 2. NoA person may not drive or move any vehicle or equipment uponon any highway with
15 anya lamp or device thereon displaying a red or green light visible from directly in front
16 of the center thereofof the vehicle or equipment. This section does not apply to anya
17 vehicle upon which a red light visible from the front is expressly authorized or required
18 by this chapter.
19 3. Flashing lights are prohibited except on an authorized:
20 a. An authorized emergency vehicle, schoolbus, snow-removal equipment;
21 b. A schoolbus;
22 c. Snow - removal equipment ;
23 d. A vehicle owned by a funeral home while used for the purpose of escorting a
24 funeral procession; or on any
Page No. 1 25.0586.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 e. Any vehicle as a means of indicating a right or left turn, or the presence of a
2 vehicular traffic hazard requiring unusual care in approaching, overtaking, or
3 passing.
