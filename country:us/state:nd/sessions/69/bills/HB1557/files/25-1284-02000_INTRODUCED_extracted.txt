Section 1:
25.1284.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representative Schatz
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the department of water resources for legal fee
2 reimbursement grants.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - DEPARTMENT OF WATER RESOURCES - LEGAL FEE
5 REIMBURSEMENT GRANTS - ONE-TIME FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any moneys
6 in the state water commissioner fund in the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum
7 of $221,993, or so much of the sum as may be necessary, to the department of water resources
8 for the purpose of providing grants to reimburse residents for legal fees associated with
9 watershed lawsuits heard by the North Dakota supreme court, for the biennium beginning
10 July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. This funding is considered a one-time funding item.
