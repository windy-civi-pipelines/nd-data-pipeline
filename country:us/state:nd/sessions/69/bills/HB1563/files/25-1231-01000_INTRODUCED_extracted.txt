Section 1:
25.1231.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Davis, Brown, Finley-DeVille, Foss, Holle, Ista
Senators Marcellais, Weston
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a legislative management study related to tribal land taxation.
2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
3 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - TRIBAL LAND TAXATION. During
4 the 2025-26 interim, the legislative management shall study tribal land taxation issues related to
5 taxation of land owned by enrolled tribal members who reside within the boundaries of any tribal
6 reservation in this state. The study may include analysis of federal law, judicial decisions
7 concerning tribal taxation authority, state property tax exemptions related to property of Native
8 Americans, and the interaction between tribal sovereignty and state law. The legislative
9 management shall report its findings and recommendations, together with any legislation
10 required to implement the recommendations, to the seventieth legislative assembly.
