Section 1:
25.0580.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Senators Roers, Cory, Rummel
Representatives Christy, Stemen, Vigesaa
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 39-04 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to blackout number plates.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 39-04 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Blackout number plates.
7 1. The director shall issue blackout number plates under this section upon receiving
8 payment of:
9 a. All other fees required under this chapter for registration of a motor vehicle;
10 b. An initial fee of fifteen dollars; and
11 c. An additional fee of twenty - five dollars per registration period .
12 2. Blackout number plates issued under this section must have:
13 a. A solid black background; and
14 b. White numbers and letters.
15 3. The director may issue blackout number plates under this section only to the owner of
16 a passenger motor vehicle, a truck the registered gross weight of which does not
17 exceed twenty thousand pounds [9071.85 kilograms], or a motorcycle.
