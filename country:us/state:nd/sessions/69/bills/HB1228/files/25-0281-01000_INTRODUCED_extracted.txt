Section 1:
25.0281.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives D. Anderson, Bahl, Vollmer
Senator Thomas
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to designating a portion of state highway 5 in Bottineau as the LCDR
3 Carl J. Woods Vietnam bridge; to provide a continuing appropriation; and to declare an
4 emergency.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
7 and enacted as follows:
8 LCDR Carl J. Woods Vietnam bridge - State highway 5 - Continuing appropriation .
9 The department shall designate the portion of highway on state highway 5 in Bottineau
10 between the junction of thompson street and the junction of brander street as the LCDR Car l J .
11 Woods Vietnam bridge and shall place signs along the highway designating that name. The
12 department may accept any appropriate signs or funds donated to the department for the
13 placement of signs. Any donated funds are appropriated to the department on a continuing
14 basis for the purpose of providing signs designating the portion of highway on state highway 5
15 as the LCDR Car l J. Woods Vietnam bridge .
16 SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
