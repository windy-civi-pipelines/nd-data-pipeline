Section 1:
25.0176.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Conference Committee Amendments
Introduced by
Appropriations Committee
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation for defraying the expenses of the state fair
2 association; to repeal section 4.1-45-22.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to state
3 fair facility operations and maintenance costs; and to declare an emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION. The funds provided in this section, or so much of the funds
6 as may be necessary, are appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state
7 treasury, not otherwise appropriated, to the state fair association for the purpose of defraying
8 the expenses of the state fair association, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending
9 June 30, 2027, as follows:
10 Adjustments or
11 Base Level Enhancements Appropriation
12 Premiums $642,833 $0 $642,833
13 Campground rest facility 0 750,000 750,000
14 Safety and security infrastructure 0 800,000 800,000
15 Total all funds $642,833 $1,550,000 $2,192,833
16 Less other funds 0 1,550,000 1,550,000
17 Total general fund $642,833 $0 $642,833
18 SECTION 2. ONE-TIME FUNDING - EFFECT ON BASE BUDGET - REPORT TO
19 SEVENTIETH LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY. The following amounts reflect the one-time funding
20 items included in the appropriation in section 1 of this Act which are not included in the entity's
21 base budget for the 2027-29 biennium and which the entity shall report to the appropriations
22 committees of the seventieth legislative assembly regarding the use of this funding:
Page No. 1 25.0176.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 One-Time Funding Description General Fund Other Funds Total
2 Campground rest facility $0 $750,000 $750,000
3 Safety and security infrastructure 0 800,000 800,000
4 Total $0 $1,550,000 $1,550,000
5 SECTION 3. OTHER FUNDS - STRATEGIC INVESTMENT AND IMPROVEMENTS FUND.
6 The other funds line item in section 1 of this Act includes the sum of $1,550,000 from the
7 strategic investment and improvements fund for a campground rest facility and for safety and
8 security infrastructure.
9 SECTION 4. CAMPGROUND REST FACILITY FUNDING - DISTRIBUTION. Funding
10 provided for the campground rest facility in section 1 of this Act is to provide a cost-share, not to
11 exceed $750,000. The cost-share provides $2 of state funding for each $1 of state fair funds.
12 SECTION 5. REPEAL. Section 4.1-45-22.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is repealed.
13 SECTION 6. EMERGENCY. The sum of $1,550,000 provided for the campground rest
14 facility and the safety and security infrastructure in section 1 of this Act is declared to be an
15 emergency measure.
