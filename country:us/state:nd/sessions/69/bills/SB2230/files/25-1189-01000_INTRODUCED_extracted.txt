Section 1:
25.1189.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Myrdal, Hogue, Meyer
Representatives McLeod, Rohr
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 16.1-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the secretary of state's duty to distribute educational and informational
3 materials before an election; and to provide an appropriation.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 16.1-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Secretary of state to distribute information to qualified electors regarding ballot
8 measures.
9 1. For each constitutional measure approved by the legislative assembly, initiated
10 measure, or referendum appearing on the ballot of a special, primary, or general
11 election, the secretary of state shall create and distribute objective and factual
12 educational materials to each individual listed as "secured active" in the central voter
13 file not less than thirty - five days before the election at which the question will be voted
14 upon.
15 2. The materials created and distributed under subsection 1 must contain :
16 a. An objective summary of the measure;
17 b. The anticipated fiscal impact of the measure;
18 c. Whether the measure was approved by the legislative assembly or originated as
19 an initiative or referred petition; and
20 d. An objective analysis of a vote in favor and a vote opposed.
21 SECTION 2. APPROPRIATION - SECRETARY OF STATE - BALLOT MEASURE
22 EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in
23 the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $250,000, or so much of the sum as
24 may be necessary, to the secretary of state for the purpose of creating and distributing objective
Page No. 1 25.1189.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 and factual educational materials relating to measures placed on a statewide election ballot as
2 provided in section 1 of this Act, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30,
3 2027.
