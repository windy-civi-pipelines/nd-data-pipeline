Section 1:
25.0956.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senator Thomas
Representatives D. Anderson, Vollmer
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the parks and recreation department for a Lake
2 Metigoshe state park sewer infrastructure replacement grant project.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - PARKS AND RECREATION - LAKE METIGOSHE
5 STATE PARK SEWER INFRASTRUCTURE REPLACEMENT GRANT PROJECT - ONE-TIME
6 FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state treasury,
7 not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $1,000,000, or so much of the sum as may be
8 necessary, to the parks and recreation department, for the purpose of providing a grant to a
9 local water resource district to replace the water and sewer infrastructure system servicing Lake
10 Metigoshe state park and surrounding areas, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and
11 ending June 30, 2027. This funding is considered a one-time funding item.
