Section 1:
25.1210.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Monson, K. Anderson, Ista, Martinson, Sanford, Swiontek
Senators Cory, Meyer, Myrdal, Weston
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the parks and recreation department for a ski
2 resort infrastructure grant.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - PARKS AND RECREATION DEPARTMENT - SKI
5 RESORT INFRASTRUCTURE GRANT - MATCHING FUNDS REQUIREMENT - ONE-TIME
6 FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the strategic investment and
7 improvements fund in the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $500,000, or so
8 much of the sum as may be necessary, to the parks and recreation department for the purpose
9 of providing a grant to a ski resort in the state for building and infrastructure repairs caused by
10 snow and rain damage, deferred maintenance, and equipment purchases, for the biennium
11 beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. The parks and recreation department may
12 spend the funds identified in this section only to the extent the organization has secured
13 matching funds from nonstate sources on a dollar-for-dollar basis. This funding is considered a
14 one-time funding item.
