Section 1:
25.0671.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Bolinske, Louser, S. Olson, Richter, D. Ruby, M. Ruby, Wolff
Senators Burckhard, Castaneda
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 2 of section 39-08-01.2 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to punishment for causing injury while operating a vehicle under the
3 influence of alcohol; and to provide a penalty.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 2 of section 39-08-01.2 of the North Dakota
6 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 2. An individual is guilty of criminal vehicular injury if the individual violates section
8 39-08-01 or an equivalent ordinance and as a result that individual causes substantial
9 bodily or serious bodily injury to another individual. Violation of this subsection is a
10 class CB felony. If an individual violates this subsection, the court shall impose at least
11 one year'syear and one day's imprisonment. If the individual violates this section after
12 having been previously convicted of a violation of section 39-08-01 or 39-08-03 or an
13 equivalent ordinance, the court shall impose at least two years' imprisonment.
