Section 1:
25.8042.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Transportation Committee
(At the request of the Department of Transportation)
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact section 24-01-12.4 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to sponsorship agreements for highway related services within highway right of way.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. Section 24-01-12.4 of the North Dakota Century Code is created and enacted
5 as follows:
6 24-01-12.4. Sponsorship agreements for highway right of way services.
7 The director may enter a sponsorship agreement with any person to provide highway
8 related services within highway right of way. The director may acknowledge a sponsor that
9 provides litter control, rest area maintenance, or other services in the highway right of way
10 under this section with an acknowledgement sign within the highway right of way. For purposes
11 of this section, "acknowledgement sign" means a sign intended only to inform the public that a
12 highway-related service has been sponsored by a person. Acknowledgement signs facing the
13 same direction may not be placed less than one mile [1.61 kilometers] apart. An
14 acknowledgement sign must meet all the requirements of the adopted manual on uniform traffic
15 control devices.
