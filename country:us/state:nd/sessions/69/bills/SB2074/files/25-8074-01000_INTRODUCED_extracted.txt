Section 1:
25.8074.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Finance and Taxation Committee
(At the request of the State Treasurer)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 1 of section 57-51.1-07.7 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to municipal infrastructure fund reporting requirements.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 1 of section 57-51.1-07.7 of the North Dakota
5 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 1. By November thirtieth of each even-numbered year, starting in 2022, a city that
7 receives a grant from the fund and has the population of at least one thousand shall
8 provide a report to the state treasurer on the use of the funding. The state treasurer
9 shall notify cities of the reporting requirement by November first of each even-
10 numbered year, starting in 2022. Upon request, the state treasurer may provide an
11 extension of up to fifteen days for a city to submit the report. The state treasurer shall
12 determine the format of the report. The report must include the amount of grant
13 funding received and spent by the city and a description of the infrastructure projects
14 completed in part or in whole with the grant funding. The state treasurer shall make
15 the reports available to the public on the state treasurer's website. A city that does not
16 provide the report in a timely manner or in the correct format is not eligible to receive a
17 grant from the fund for a period of two years starting from the date the report was due.
18 If a city uses the funding in a manner inconsistent with the requirements of this section
19 as identified in any financial audits conducted by the state auditor or an independent
20 accounting firm, the state treasurer shall reduce any future grants to that city by the
21 amount spent that was inconsistent with the requirements.
