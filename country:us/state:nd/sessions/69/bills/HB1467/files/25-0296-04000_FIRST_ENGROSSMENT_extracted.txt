Section 1:
25.0296.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representative D. Anderson
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 23-12-22 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to the publication of vaccine adverse event data and information; and to provide for a
3 legislative management report.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 23-12-22 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 amended and reenacted as follows:
7 23-12-22. Vaccine adverse event data - Website.
8 The department of health and human services shall prominently:
9 1. Prominently display, on a website maintained by the department, a link to the federal
10 vaccine adverse event reporting system, along with instructions on how to report an
11 adverse event and how to access state-specific data. The department may compile its
12 own data for use on this website.; and
13 2. By July first of each even - numbered year, provide a report to the legislative
14 management of all state-specific data and a summary of all reported data nationally.
