Section 1:
25.1108.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Mitskog, Schreiber-Beck
Senators Sorvaag, Luick
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the North Dakota state college of science; and
2 to declare an emergency.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - NORTH DAKOTA STATE COLLEGE OF SCIENCE -
5 ONE-TIME FUNDING. There is appropriated from other funds derived from gifts, grants,
6 donations, and other local funds, the sum of $1,750,000, or so much of the sum as may be
7 necessary, to the North Dakota state college of science for the purpose of constructing an
8 artificial turf playing surface, for the period beginning with the effective date of this Act, and
9 ending June 30, 2027. The funding appropriated in this section includes up to $250,000 from
10 dining services auxiliary revenues. The funding appropriated in this section is considered a
11 one-time funding item.
12 SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
