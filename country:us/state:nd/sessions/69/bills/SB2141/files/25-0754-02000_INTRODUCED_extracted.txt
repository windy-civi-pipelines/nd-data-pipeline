Section 1:
25.0754.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Patten, Erbele, Kessel
Representatives Dressler, Hagert, Novak
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 61-04-15.4 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to a change in the purpose of use of a conditional or perfected water permit.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 61-04-15.4 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 61-04-15.4. Change in purpose of use.
7 1. A permitholder may change the purpose of use of a conditional or perfected water
8 permit without affecting the priority date, if approved by the department of water
9 resources. Applications for a
10 2. An application to change in the purpose of use must be processed and evaluated in
11 the same manner as a conditional water permit application. A
12 3. The department of water resources may approve the proposed change if the proposed
13 change will not adversely affect the rights of other appropriators and if a change in the
14 purpose of use may be authorized only foris:
15 a. To a superior use as determined byunder the order of priority in section
16 61-04-06.1. The department may approve the proposed change if the proposed
17 change will not adversely affect the rights of other appropriators; or
18 b. To livestock use or fish, wildlife, and recreation use for a reservoir authorizing no
19 withdrawal of water.
