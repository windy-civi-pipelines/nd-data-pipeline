Section 1:
25.0963.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Rohr, Lefor, D. Ruby, Steiner, Vigesaa
Senators Gerhardt, Hogue, Klein, Myrdal
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 43-17-27.1 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to the development of an instructional course by the state board
3 of medicine; to provide an appropriation; and to declare an emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new subsection to section 43-17-27.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 created and enacted as follows:
7 The board shall develop, in consultation with and with final approval from the attorney
8 general, an instructional course on chapters 12.1 - 19.1, 14 - 02.1, and 14 - 02.6 as the
9 chapters relate to the practice of medicine. The board shall require a physician
10 engaging in the practice of obstetrics to complete the course as part of licensure or
11 renewal requirements under this section.
12 SECTION 2. APPROPRIATION - STATE BOARD OF MEDICINE - INSTRUCTIONAL
13 COURSE DEVELOPMENT. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the
14 state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $50,000, or so much of the sum as may
15 be necessary, to the state board of medicine for the purpose of developing an instructional
16 course on chapters 12.1-19.1, 14-02.1, and 14-02.6 as the chapters relate to the practice of
17 medicine, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027.
18 SECTION 3. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
