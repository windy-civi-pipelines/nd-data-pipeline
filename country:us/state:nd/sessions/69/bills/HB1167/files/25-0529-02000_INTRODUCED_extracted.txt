Section 1:
25.0529.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Warrey, Bahl, Christianson, Christy, Dobervich, Vollmer
Senators Meyer, Weber, Sickler
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 16.1-10 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to artificial intelligence disclosure statements.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 16.1-10 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Use of artificial intelligence - Disclosure - Definition.
7 Any action taken for a political purpose, including communication and political advertising in
8 support of or opposition to a candidate, political committee, or a political party, or for the
9 purpose of promoting passage or defeat of initiated or referred measures or petitions,
10 containing images, graphics, videos, audio, text, or other digital content created in whole or in
11 part with the use of artificial intelligence, must prominently state the following disclaimer: "This
12 content generated by artificial intelligence." For purposes of this section, "artificial intelligence"
13 means a machine-based system capable of creating images, graphics, videos, audio, text, and
14 other digital content based on human defined objectives or data patterns. The term does not
15 include systems that are explicitly programmed with rules.
