Section 1:
25.1261.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Erbele, Klein, Lemm
Representatives Fisher, Grueneich, Hauck
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 4.1-28-02 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to the authority to transact business associated with video livestock auction markets;
3 and to repeal sections 4.1-28-03 and 4.1-28-04 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to
4 licensing and fees for video livestock auction market representatives.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 4.1-28-02 of the North Dakota Century Code is
7 amended and reenacted as follows:
8 4.1-28-02. Video livestock auction market and internet auction - Authority to transact
9 business.
10 A video livestock auction market or internet livestock auction market may not transact
11 business in this state unless the market transacts business through a representative licensed
12 under this chapter.
13 SECTION 2. REPEAL. Sections 4.1-28-03 and 4.1-28-04 of the North Dakota Century
14 Code are repealed.
