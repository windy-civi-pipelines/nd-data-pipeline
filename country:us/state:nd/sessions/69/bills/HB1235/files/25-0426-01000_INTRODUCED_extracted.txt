Section 1:
25.0426.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Grueneich, Brandenburg, Dockter, Hagert, Headland, Richter, Satrom,
Stemen
Senators Conley, Erbele, Thomas, Wanzek
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 2 of section 4.1-01-27 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the agriculture infrastructure grant program.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 2 of section 4.1-01-27 of the North Dakota Century
5 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 2. The agriculture commissioner, in consultation with the director of the department of
7 transportation, shall award grants to political subdivisions for road and bridge
8 improvements necessary to accommodate value-added agriculture businesses. Grant
9 funding under this subsection may be used for:
10 a. Corridor improvements on city, county, and township roadways; and
11 b. Improvements to roads or bridges that provide access to value-added agriculture
12 businesses.
