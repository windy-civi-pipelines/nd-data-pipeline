Section 1:
25.0715.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Schaible, Luick, Myrdal
Representatives Holle, Kempenich, Beltz
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a legislative management study relating to the creation of a farm
2 safety insurance discount program.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - AGRICULTURE SAFETY COURSE
5 TO REDUCE INSURANCE PREMIUMS. During the 2025-26 interim, the legislative
6 management shall study the feasibility and desirability of creating a program that would allow a
7 farmer or rancher to complete a safety course to receive a discount on insurance relating to the
8 individual's farming or ranching operation. The study must include the development of a
9 proposed safety course, the feasible amount of an insurance premium discount, and eligibility
10 requirements for applicants seeking to participate in the discount program. The study must
11 include input from the insurance commissioner, agriculture commissioner, the North Dakota
12 state university extension service, and farm, livestock, and agricultural insurance agencies
13 operating in the state. The legislative management shall report its findings and
14 recommendations, together with any legislation required to implement the recommendations, to
15 the seventieth legislative assembly.
