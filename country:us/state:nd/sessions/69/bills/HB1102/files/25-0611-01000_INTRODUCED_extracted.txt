Section 1:
25.0611.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Murphy, Christianson, Hagert, Ista, O'Brien, Osowski
Senators Barta, Sickler
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the attorney general for bureau of criminal
2 investigation cybercrime resources.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - ATTORNEY GENERAL - BUREAU OF CRIMINAL
5 INVESTIGATION CYBERCRIME RESOURCES - FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT POSITIONS -
6 ONE-TIME FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state
7 treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $775,000, or so much of the sum as may be
8 necessary, to the attorney general for the purpose of hiring two full-time equivalent bureau of
9 criminal investigation cybercrime agent positions and for related equipment and capital asset
10 costs, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. The personnel hired
11 and resources purchased pursuant to this section must be dedicated to assisting a police
12 department in a city in northeastern North Dakota with at least 50,000 residents with the
13 prevention and detection of cybercrime. Of the amount appropriated in this section, $161,000 is
14 considered a one-time funding item for the purchase of equipment.
