Section 1:
25.0755.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Clemens, Boehm
Representatives K. Anderson, Frelich, D. Ruby
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 14-09-06.5 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to additional parenting time after a false allegation of harm to a child.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 14-09-06.5 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 14-09-06.5. Allegation of harm to child - Effect.
7 1. If the court finds that an allegation of harm to a child by one parent against the other is
8 false and not made in good faith, the court shall order the parent making the false
9 allegation to pay court costs and reasonable attorney's fees incurred by the other
10 parent in responding to the allegation.
11 2. Unless a party shows good cause why additional parenting time should not be
12 ordered, if the court finds an allegation of harm to a child by one parent against the
13 other is false and not made in good faith, a court shall order additional parenting time
14 to the parent responding to the false allegation to indemnify that parent for any denial
15 of court-ordered parenting time that resulted from an investigation by any person
16 which did not result in a finding of harm, abuse, or neglect.
17 3. The additional parenting time:
18 a. Must be of the same type and duration of parenting time that was denied;
19 b. May include weekend, holiday, and summer parenting time; and
20 c. Must occur within two years of the date the court finds the allegation was false
21 and not made in good faith.
22 4. A parent entitled to additional parenting time under subsection 2 may determine the
23 schedule of the additional parenting time subject to subsection 3 .
