Section 1:
25.8003.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Workforce Development Committee
(At the request of the North Dakota Board of Medicine)
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 43-58-03.1 and section
2 43-60-06.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to obtaining criminal history record
3 checks for naturopathic doctors and genetic counselors; and to amend and reenact
4 subdivision d of subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 of the North Dakota Century Code, providing
5 authority to complete the background checks with the bureau of criminal investigation to
6 complete the criminal history record checks.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
8 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subdivision d of subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 of the North
9 Dakota Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
10 d. The North Dakota board of medicine for licenses or disciplinary investigations
11 under sectionsections 43-17-07.1 , 43 - 58 - 03.1, and 43 - 60 - 06.1 , except that
12 criminal history record checks need not be made unless required by the board.
13 SECTION 2. A new subsection to section 43-58-03.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is
14 created and enacted as follows:
15 The board may require an applicant for licensure or a licensee who is the subject of a
16 disciplinary investigation to submit to a statewide and nationwide criminal history
17 record check. The nationwide criminal history record check must be conducted in the
18 manner provided by section 12 - 60 - 24. All costs associated with the criminal history
19 record check are the responsibility of the licensee or applicant.
20 SECTION 3. Section 43-60-06.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is created and enacted
21 as follows:
22 43-60-06.1. Criminal history record checks.
23 The board may require an applicant for licensure or a licensee who is the subject of a
24 disciplinary investigation to submit to a statewide and nationwide criminal history record check.
