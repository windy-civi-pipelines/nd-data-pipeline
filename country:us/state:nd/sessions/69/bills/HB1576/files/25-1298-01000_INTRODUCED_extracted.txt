Section 1:
25.1298.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Kempenich, B. Anderson, Headland, Steiner
Senators Bekkedahl, Kessel, Rummel, Hogue
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a legislative management study of the oil and gas tax revenue
2 allocation formulas.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - OIL AND GAS TAX REVENUE
5 ALLOCATION FORMULAS. During the 2025-26 interim, the legislative management shall
6 consider studying the oil and gas tax revenue allocation formulas. The study must include
7 consideration of current and historical allocations to political subdivisions and the appropriate
8 level of oil and gas tax revenue allocations to political subdivisions based on infrastructure and
9 other oil- and gas-related development impact needs. The legislative management shall report
10 its findings and recommendations, together with any legislation required to implement the
11 recommendations, to the seventieth legislative assembly.
