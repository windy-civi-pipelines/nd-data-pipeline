Section 1:
Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly of North Dakota
HOUSE BILL NO. 1039
(Human Services Committee)
(At the request of the North Dakota Board of Medicine)
AN ACT to amend and reenact subsection 4 of section 43-17.3-01 of the North Dakota Century Code,
relating to the definition of licensee eligible for participation in the physician health program.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 4 of section 43-17.3-01 of the North Dakota Century Code
is amended and reenacted as follows:

Section 3:
4. "Licensee" means a physician or other health professional under the jurisdiction of the board,
and includes an applicant for licensure or regulation by the board and former licensees of the
board.
