Section 1:
25.8001.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Human Services Committee
(At the request of the North Dakota Board of Medicine)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 4 of section 43-17.3-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the definition of licensee eligible for participation in the physician
3 health program.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 4 of section 43-17.3-01 of the North Dakota
6 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 4. "Licensee" means a physician or other health professional under the jurisdiction of the
8 board, and includes an applicant for licensure or regulation by the board and former
9 licensees of the board.
