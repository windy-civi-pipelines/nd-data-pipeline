25.0275.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Senate Amendments
Introduced by
Representatives Dockter, O'Brien, M. Ruby, Weisz
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a legislative management study relating to early discontinuation
2 of registration for low-risk sex offenders.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - EARLY DISCONTINUATION OF
5 REGISTRATION FOR LOW-RISK SEX OFFENDERS.
6 1. During the 2025-26 interim, the legislative management shall study the impacts to the
7 public, victims, the sex offender risk assessment committee, law enforcement
8 agencies, sex offenders, and other relevant parties, of allowing some low-risk sex
9 offenders to petition the court for an order for early discontinuation relieving the
10 offender of the duty to register. The study must include a thorough analysis of
11 establishing a framework for submitting and reviewing petitions with the inclusion of a
12 victim advocate if the early discontinuation of registration requirements is
13 recommended by the committee. The study also must include an analysis of:
14 a. The effect of early discontinuation of registration on public safety and victim
15 safety.
16 b. The length of registration which should be required before a low-risk sex offender
17 would qualify to petition for early discontinuation of registration.
18 c. Registrable criminal offenses that should make a low-risk sex offender eligible or
19 ineligible to petition for early discontinuation of registration.
20 d. How the age of a victim and the age of the victim compared to the offender would
21 impact eligibility to petition for early discontinuation of registration.
Page No. 1 25.0275.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 e. Whether the offender's need for end-of-life care would impact eligibility to petition
2 for early discontinuation of registration.
3 f. Any other relevant factors.
4 2. The legislative management shall report its findings and recommendations, together
5 with any legislation required to implement the recommendations, to the Seventieth
6 Legislative Assembly.
