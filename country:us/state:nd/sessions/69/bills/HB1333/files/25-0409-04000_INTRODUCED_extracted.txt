Section 1:
25.0409.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Heilman, Hauck, Jonas, Maki, Marschall, Morton, Osowski
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 15.1-19 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the duration of a student's school lunch break.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 15.1-19 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Students - Duration of lunch.
7 1. A school district shall provide each student at least thirty minutes per day for a lunch
8 break, which must take place between ten a.m. and two p.m .
9 2. A high school student must be excused from the class immediately following the
10 student's lunch break for the number of minutes, if any, necessary to satisfy
11 subsection 1 each day of that semester, if :
12 a. The student obtains a written document from a school counselor at the high
13 school verifying the student may be excused under this section; and
14 b. Provides the document to the teacher of the affected class once per semester.
