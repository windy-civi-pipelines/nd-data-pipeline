Section 1:
25.0593.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senator Magrum
Representatives S. Olson, Hendrix
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 54-05.1 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to restrictions on legislative lobbyists; to amend and reenact section
3 54-05.1-07 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to penalties for legislative lobbyists; and
4 to provide a penalty.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 54-05.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
7 and enacted as follows:
8 Lobbyists prohibited from entering legislative chambers - Exception .
9 An individual required to register under this chapter is prohibited from entering the
10 chambers of the house of representatives or the senate during a regular or special session of
11 the legislative assembly unless the individual:
12 1. Is present in either the balconies of the house of representatives or the senate; or
13 2. Receives written permission from the majority leader of the chamber to enter the
14 majority leader's office.
15 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Section 54-05.1-07 of the North Dakota Century Code is
16 amended and reenacted as follows:
17 54-05.1-07. Penalty.
18 Any personAn individual who violates any provisionsprovision of this chapter is guilty of a
19 class B misdemeanor except that a violation of section 54-05.1-02 or, 54-05.1-03 , or section 1
20 of this Act is an infraction. Whether a personan individual is subjected to criminal prosecution
21 under this section, and in addition to the registration fee that may be assessed when the
22 personindividual submits the registration to the secretary of state, the personindividual may be
23 assessed a civil penalty by the secretary of state, following written notice to the personindividual
24 of an intent to assess the penalty, in an amount not to exceed two times the amount set forth in
Page No. 1 25.0593.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 subdivision e of subsection 1 of section 54-05.1-03 which is chargeable to a lobbyist. Any civil
2 penalty must be assessed and collected before a personan individual is issued a certificate of
3 registration. The assessment of a civil penalty may be appealed to the district court of the
4 person'sindividual's county of residence or Burleigh County, but only on the basis that the
5 secretary of state's administrative determination that the personindividual acted as a lobbyist
6 when not registered as a lobbyist was clearly erroneous.
