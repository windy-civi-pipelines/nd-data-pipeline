Section 1:
25.0802.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Schreiber-Beck, Frelich, Hanson, Hagert, Lefor, Murphy, Nathe, Mitskog
Senators Hogan, Burckhard, Meyer
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the state board of higher education for
2 workforce education innovation funds.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - STATE BOARD OF HIGHER EDUCTION -
5 WORKFORCE EDUCATION INNOVATION PROGRAM. There is appropriated out of any
6 moneys in the general fund in the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of
7 $24,000,000, or so much of the sum as may be necessary, to the state board of higher
8 education for the purpose of awarding workforce education innovation funds to eligible
9 institutions, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027.
