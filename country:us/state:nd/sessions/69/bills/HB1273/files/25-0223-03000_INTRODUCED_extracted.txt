Section 1:
25.0223.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Heilman, Bolinske, Kasper, Koppelman, Morton, Toman, Marschall
Senators Castaneda, Clemens, Van Oosting, Walen, Powers
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 54-06 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the prohibition of a policy, order, or ordinance that limits free speech.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 54-06 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Prohibition on limitation of free speech.
7 Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the state or a political subdivision may not adopt
8 or implement a policy, order, or ordinance that limits or prohibits speech of an individual,
9 including hate crime ordinances. This section may not be superseded or modified by a city or
10 county home rule charter or ordinance. A policy, order, or ordinance that violates this section is
11 void.
