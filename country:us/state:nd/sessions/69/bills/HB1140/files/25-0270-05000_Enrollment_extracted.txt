Section 1:
Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly of North Dakota
HOUSE BILL NO. 1140
(Representatives Steiner, Lefor, M. Ruby, Schatz, Kempenich, Pyle)
(Senators Bekkedahl, Dever, Hogue, Kessel, Rummel)
AN ACT to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating
to designating the bridge on state highway 22 in Dickinson as the Specialist Jon P. Fettig Iraq
bridge; to provide a continuing appropriation; and to declare an emergency.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created and
enacted as follows:
Specialist Jon P. Fettig Iraq bridge - State highway 22 - Continuing appropriation .
The department shall designate the bridge on state highway 22 in Dickinson between the junction
of Kuchenski drive and the junction of fourth street southwest as the Specialist Jon P. Fettig Iraq bridge
and shall place signs along the highway designating that name. The department may accept any
appropriate signs or funds donated to the department for the placement of signs. Any donated funds are
appropriated to the department on a continuing basis for the purpose of providing signs designating the
bridge on state highway 22 as the Specialist Jon P. Fettig Iraq bridge.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
