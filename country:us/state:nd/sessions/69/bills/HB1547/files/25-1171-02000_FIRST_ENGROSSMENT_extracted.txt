Section 1:
25.1171.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Richter, Conmy, Jonas, Mitskog, Nelson, Novak, Schreiber-Beck
Senators Axtman, Beard, Davison, Mathern
1 A BILL for an Act to create a committee to study special education funding during the 2025-26
2 interim; and to provide for a legislative management report.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. SPECIAL EDUCATION FUNDING COMMITTEE - REPORT TO
5 LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT.
6 1. During the 2025-26 interim, the legislative management shall establish and provide
7 staffing and administrative services to a committee to study special education funding.
8 The chairman of legislative management may add temporary, nonvoting members to
9 the committee, as deemed necessary by the committee, to serve without
10 compensation.
11 2. The committee may include:
12 a. Eight members of the legislative assembly, four members from the house of
13 representatives and four members from the senate, appointed by the legislative
14 management;
15 b. Two multi-unit special education directors;
16 c. One large school special education director;
17 d. One special education coordinator;
18 e. One superintendent of a large school;
19 f. One superintendent of a small or midsize school;
20 g. One business manager of a large school; and
21 h. One multi-unit special education business manager.
Page No. 1 25.1171.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 3. During the 2025-26 interim, the special education funding committee study must
2 include a comprehensive review of special education funding models, including
3 student contracts, high-cost students, residential placement, and reimbursement under
4 the Medicaid program.
5 4. The committee shall report its findings and recommendations, together with any
6 legislation necessary to implement the recommendations, to the seventieth legislative
7 assembly.
