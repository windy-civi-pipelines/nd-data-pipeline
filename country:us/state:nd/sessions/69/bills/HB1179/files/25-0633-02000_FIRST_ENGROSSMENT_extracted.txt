Section 1:
25.0633.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Murphy, Conmy, Hager, Jonas, Longmuir, Richter, Schreiber-Beck
Senator Cleary
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a legislative management study regarding paid time off for the
2 faculty at an institution of higher education.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - PAID TIME OFF FOR FACULTY OF
5 AN INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION. During the 2025-26 interim, the legislative
6 management shall consider studying requiring each institution of higher education to provide
7 paid time off to faculty members appointed to at least a twelve-month term of employment. The
8 study must include a comprehensive review of in-state and out-of-state leave policies for
9 institutions of higher education. The legislative management shall report its findings and
10 recommendations, together with any legislation necessary to implement the recommendations,
11 to the seventieth legislative assembly.
