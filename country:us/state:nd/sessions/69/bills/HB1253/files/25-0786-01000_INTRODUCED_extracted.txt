Section 1:
25.0786.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Grueneich, Dockter, Koppelman, J. Olson, Stemen, Wagner
Senators Conley, Meyer, Roers
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 10 of section 53-06.1-15.1 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to the authority of the attorney general to impose fines for
3 gaming violations.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 10 of section 53-06.1-15.1 of the North Dakota
6 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 10. Impose a monetary fine on a licensed organization, organization that has a permit,
8 distributor, manufacturer, owner of an authorized site, or third-party business operating
9 gaming and working as an agent of the charity for failure to comply with this chapter or
10 any gaming rule. This fine may be in addition to or in lieu of a license suspension or
11 revocation. The monetary fine for each violation by an:
12 a. An organization is a minimum of twenty-five dollars and may not exceed two
13 percent of the organization's average quarterly gross proceeds, or five thousand
14 dollars, whichever is greater. The monetary fine for each violation by a
15 b. A distributor is a minimum of one hundred dollars and may not exceed five
16 thousand dollars. The monetary fine for each violation by a
17 c. A manufacturer is a minimum of five hundred dollars and may not exceed two
18 hundred fifty thousand dollars. This fine may be in addition to or in place of a
19 license suspension or revocation.
20 d. An owner of an authorized site is a minimum of two hundred fifty dollars and may
21 not exceed two thousand five hundred dollars.
