Section 1:
25.0487.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Schatz, Hauck, Holle, Kempenich, Monson, Dressler
Senators Boehm, Magrum, Paulson, Wanzek
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 19-02.1-12.1 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to cell-cultured protein; and to repeal section 4.1-31-05.1 of the North Dakota
3 Century Code, relating to the misrepresentation of cell-cultured protein.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 19-02.1-12.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 amended and reenacted as follows:
7 19-02.1-12.1. Misrepresentation of cellCell-cultured protein as meat food product
8 prohibited.
9 1. A person may not advertisemanufacture for sale, offer for sale, or sell, or misrepresent
10 cell-cultured protein as a meat food product. A cell-cultured food product:
11 a. May not be packaged in the same, or deceptively similar, packaging as a meat
12 food product; and
13 b. Must be labeled as a cell-cultured food productin this state.
14 2. For purposes of this section, "deceptively similar" means packaging that could mislead
15 a reasonable person to believe the product is a meat food product"cell-cultured
16 protein" means a food product developed in a lab and grown from a sample of animal
17 cells which does not require the slaughter of animals.
18 SECTION 2. REPEAL. Section 4.1-31-05.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is repealed.
