Section 1:
25.0561.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Cleary, Meyer, Thomas
Representatives Bahl, Monson, Nelson
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 13 of section 1-01-49, subsection 4 of
2 section 10-30.5-01, and section 40-57.1-02 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the
3 definition of primary sector business.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 13 of section 1-01-49 of the North Dakota Century
6 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
7 13. "Primary sector business" means an individual, corporation, limited liability company,
8 partnership, or association certified by the department of commerce division of
9 economic development and finance which through the employment of knowledge or
10 labor adds value to a product, process, or service which results in the creation of new
11 wealth. The term includes tourism operations demonstrating the creation of new
12 wealth by attracting ou t - o f - state visitors .
13 For purposes of this subsection, "new wealth" means revenues generated by a
14 business or tourism operation in this state through the sale of products or services to:
15 a. Customers or visitors from outside of this state; or
16 b. Customers in this state if the products or services were previously unavailable or
17 difficult to obtain from a business in this state.
18 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Subsection 4 of section 10-30.5-01 of the North Dakota
19 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
20 4. "Primary sector business" has the meaning provided in section 1-01-49 and includes
21 tourism but does not include production agriculture.
22 SECTION 3. AMENDMENT. Section 40-57.1-02 of the North Dakota Century Code is
23 amended and reenacted as follows:
Page No. 1 25.0561.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 40-57.1-02. Definitions.
2 As used in this chapter, unless a different meaning clearly appears from the context:
3 1. "Local development organization", as used in section 40-57.1-04.3, means a profit or
4 nonprofit corporation incorporated under the laws of this state or a limited liability
5 company organized under the laws of this state, formed for the purpose of furthering
6 the economic development of its community and environs, with authority to promote
7 and assist the growth and development of business concerns in the areas covered by
8 its operations. The operations of the corporation or limited liability company must be
9 limited to a specified area in this state. The controlling interest in the corporation or
10 limited liability company must be held by at least twenty-five persons residing or doing
11 business in the community or its environs. These persons must control not less than
12 seventy-five percent of the voting control of the corporation or limited liability company.
13 NoA shareholder or member of the corporation or limited liability company may not
14 own in excess of twenty-five percent of the voting control in the corporation or limited
15 liability company if that shareholder or member has a direct pecuniary interest in any
16 project or business concern whichthat will occupy the property of the corporation or
17 limited liability company. The primary objective of the corporation or limited liability
18 company must be to benefit the community through increased employment, payroll,
19 business volume, and corresponding factors rather than monetary profits to its
20 shareholders or members. Any monetary profits or other benefits going to the
21 shareholders or members must be merely incidental to the primary objective of the
22 corporation or limited liability company.
23 2. "Municipality" means counties as well as municipalities of the types listed in
24 subsection 4 of section 40-01-01.
25 3. "Primary sector business" has the meaning provided in section 1-01-49.
26 4. "Project" means any revenue-producing enterprise, or any combination of two or more
27 of these enterprises. For the purpose of the income tax exemption, "project" means
28 both "primary sector business" and "tourism" as defined by this section and includes
29 the establishment of a new qualifying business or the expansion of a qualifying
30 existing business.

2 historical and cultural events, guide services, and unique lodging and food services
3 which serve as destination attractions.
