Section 1:
25.1172.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Senate Amendments
Introduced by
Representatives M. Ruby, Fisher, Heinert, Nathe, Stemen, D. Ruby
Senators Castaneda, Cleary, Meyer, Paulson, Thomas
1 A BILL for an Act to authorize the department of transportation to transfer real property to the
2 office of management and budget; to provide an appropriation; to authorize a line of credit; to
3 provide an exemption; and to declare an emergency.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. TRANSFER OF LAND AUTHORIZED. The state of North Dakota, by and
6 through the department of transportation, shall convey certain real property consisting of
7 approximately one and one-quarter acres [.51 hectare] in the southwest corner of outlot 20 in
8 the south half of the north half of the northwest quarter of section twenty-eight, township
9 one hundred fifty-five north, range eighty-two west, Ward County, North Dakota, to the office of
10 management and budget. Sections 54-01-05.2 and 54-01-05.5 do not apply to the transfer
11 authorized by this section.
12 SECTION 2. APPROPRIATION - OFFICE OF MANAGEMENT AND BUDGET - MINOT
13 BUILDING - LINE OF CREDIT - ONE-TIME FUNDING - EXEMPTION. The office of
14 management and budget may borrow up to $5,600,000 through a line of credit from the Bank of
15 North Dakota during the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027, the
16 proceeds of which are appropriated to the office of management and budget for the purpose of
17 constructing a facility in Minot to lease to other state agencies. The interest rate on the line of
18 credit may not exceed the prevailing interest rate charged to North Dakota governmental
19 entities. If the office of management and budget accesses the line of credit, the office of
20 management and budget shall request a deficiency appropriation from the seventieth legislative
21 assembly to repay the line of credit. The appropriation in this section is considered a one-time
Page No. 1 25.1172.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 funding item. The appropriation in this section is not subject to section 54-44.1-11 and may be
2 continued into the biennium beginning July 1, 2027, and ending June 30, 2029.
3 SECTION 3. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
