20 order:

24 sinking and interest fund under section 6 - 09.4 - 10.1 .
25 b. The next one hundred million dollars to the legacy earnings highway distribution
26 fund for allocations under section 54 - 27 - 19.3 .
27 c. The remaining amount to the legacy property tax relief fund under section 5 o f

5 SECTION 9. A new section to chapter 57-02 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Residential, agricultural, and commercial property valuation reduction -
8 Qualification - Certification - State reimbursement .
9 1. A taxpayer is entitled to a reduction of the taxable valuation of the taxpayer's
10 residential property, agricultural property, or commercial property as provided in this
11 section. The reduction is equal to:
12 a. For residential property, two and three-fourths percent of the assessed value of
13 the property.
14 b. For agricultural property and commercial property, one and one - half percent o f
15 the assessed value of the property.
16 2. The reduction under this section must be applied before other credits under this
17 chapter.
18 3. Persons who are co-owners of the property but who are not spouses or dependents
19 each are entitled to a percentage of a full reduction under subsection 1 equal to their
20 ownership interests in the property.
21 4. Determinations of eligibility for a reduction under this section may be appealed through
22 the equalization and abatement process.
23 5. The county auditor shall apply the reduction under this section on the corresponding
24 property tax or mobile home tax statement.
25 6. This section does not reduce the liability of any owner for special assessments levied
26 upon any property.
27 7. A reduction of taxable valuation under this section may not be applied to reduce the
28 taxable valuation used for purposes of determining the amount subtracted from a
29 school district's state aid payment under subdivision a of subsection 4 of section
30 15.1 - 27 - 04.1 .
