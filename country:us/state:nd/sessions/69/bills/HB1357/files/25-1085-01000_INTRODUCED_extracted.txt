Section 1:
25.1085.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Morton, Kasper, Maki, Marschall, Osowski, Schauer, Schreiber-Beck,
McLeod, K. Anderson
Senators Boschee, Van Oosting
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 15.1-07-25.3 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to protection of student data.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 15.1-07-25.3 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 15.1-07-25.3. Protection of student data - School district policy.
7 1. The board of eacha school district shall adopt a policy regarding the protection of
8 student data.
9 2. The policy must require:
10 a. Require that permission be obtained from the board before any student data is
11 shared with an individual who is not a school district employee or shared with any
12 other entity. This provision does not apply to the sharing of data with a student's
13 parent or to the sharing of data, ifas otherwise required by law.
14 b. Permit a school district to share student data with a state entity using a student
15 information system or the state longitudinal dashboard system if the school
16 district and the state entity have entered a data sharing agreement.
17 3. The policy must require the school district superintendent to compile:
18 a. A list of all individuals with whom, and entities with which, student data is shared;
19 and
20 b. A list, by title, of all school district personnel who have access to student data.
21 4. A school district shall make copies of the policy available upon request.
