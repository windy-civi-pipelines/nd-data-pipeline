Section 1:
25.1032.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
with Senate Amendments
Introduced by
Representatives Klemin, Fegley, Warrey
Senator Barta
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 44-04 and a new section to
2 chapter 58-03 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to an open meeting exemption for a
3 board of township supervisors when conducting an onsite inspection.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 44-04 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Township onsite inspections - Exemption .
8 An onsite inspection conducted under section 2 of this Act is an exempt meeting .
9 SECTION 2. A new section to chapter 58-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
10 and enacted as follows:
11 Onsite inspections - Exempt meeting .
12 1. A gathering of the members of a board of township supervisors to conduct an onsite
13 inspection under this title is an exempt meeting as defined under section 44 - 04 - 17.1 if :
14 a. The township does not have staff available that is capable of making the
15 inspection; or
16 b. The board of township supervisors is serving as the township officers and the
17 staff of the township.
18 2. The board of township supervisors shall make good-faith efforts to provide notice of
19 the inspections to each news medium that has filed a written request for notice if the
20 request includes the news medium's telephone number. The notice must be given by
21 telephone or by any other method used to notify the members of the public body.
