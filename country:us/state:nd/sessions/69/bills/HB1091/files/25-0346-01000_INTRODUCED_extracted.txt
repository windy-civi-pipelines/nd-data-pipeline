Section 1:
25.0346.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives K. Anderson, D. Anderson, J. Olson, M. Ruby, Wagner
Senators Barta, Lemm, Myrdal
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to designating the bridge on United States highway 81 as the
3 Specialist 4 David LaTraille Vietnam bridge; to provide a continuing appropriation; and to
4 declare an emergency.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
7 and enacted as follows:
8 Specialist 4 David LaTraille Vietnam bridge - United States highway 81 - Continuing
9 appropriation.
10 The department shall designate the bridge on United States highway 81 in Grafton between
11 the junction of park street and the junction of Cashel street as the Specialis t 4 David LaTraille
12 Vietnam bridge and shall place signs along the highway designating that name. The department
13 may accept any appropriate signs or funds donated to the department for the placement of
14 signs. Any donated funds are appropriated to the department on a continuing basis for the
15 purpose of providing signs designating the bridge on United States highway 81 as the
16 Specialis t 4 David LaTraille Vietnam bridge .
17 SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
