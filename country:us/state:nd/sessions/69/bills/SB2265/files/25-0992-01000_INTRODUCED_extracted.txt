Section 1:
25.0992.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Meyer, Burckhard, Hogan, Luick, Sorvaag
Representative Schauer
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the department of veterans' affairs for a
2 veterans' national cemetery grant.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - STRATEGIC INVESTMENT AND IMPROVEMENTS
5 FUND - DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS' AFFAIRS - VETERANS' NATIONAL CEMETERY
6 GRANT - ONE-TIME FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the strategic
7 investment and improvements fund in the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of
8 $3,000,000, or so much of the sum as may be necessary, to the department of veterans' affairs,
9 for the purpose of providing a grant to a nonprofit organization for construction costs associated
10 with the construction of a chapel, parking lot, and related infrastructure at a veterans' national
11 cemetery in the state, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. The
12 funding provided in this section is considered a one-time funding item.
