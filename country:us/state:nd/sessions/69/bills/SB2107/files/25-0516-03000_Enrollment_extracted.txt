Section 1:
Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly of North Dakota
SENATE BILL NO. 2107
(Senator Beard)
AN ACT to amend and reenact subsection 11 of section 50-11.1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code,
relating to early childhood services training for licensure; and to provide an effective date.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 11 of section 50-11.1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code
is amended and reenacted as follows:

Section 3:
11. Any hours of department-approved training related to child care which an applicant or staff
member completes after submitting the fees and application as required under subsection 8
must be counted toward the licensing annual requirements for the following year.

Section 4:
SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. This Act becomes effective on January 1, 2026.
