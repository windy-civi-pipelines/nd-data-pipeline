Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly of North Dakota
HOUSE BILL NO. 1081
(Government and Veterans Affairs Committee)
(At the request of the Office of Management and Budget)
AN ACT to repeal sections 54-27-21 and 54-27-21.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to fixed
asset minimum reporting value and documentation of asset acquisitions; and to declare an
emergency.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. REPEAL. Sections 54-27-21 and 54-27-21.1 of the North Dakota Century Code are
repealed.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.

Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly of North Dakota
In Regular Session Commencing Tuesday, January 7, 2025
HOUSE BILL NO. 1081
(Government and Veterans Affairs Committee)
(At the request of the Office of Management and Budget)
AN ACT to repeal sections 54-27-21 and 54-27-21.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to fixed
asset minimum reporting value and documentation of asset acquisitions; and to declare an
emergency.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
SECTION 1. REPEAL. Sections 54-27-21 and 54-27-21.1 of the North Dakota Century Code are
repealed.
SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
