1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 62.1-04-04 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to producing a license to carry a concealed firearm upon request from a law
3 enforcement officer; and to provide a penalty.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 62.1-04-04 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 amended and reenacted as follows:
7 62.1-04-04. Producing license upon request - Penalty.
8 1. EveryAn individual while carrying a concealed firearm or dangerous weapon, for which
9 a license to carry concealed is required, shall have on one'sthe individual's person the
10 license issued by this or another state or a digital image of one'sthe individual's
11 concealed firearm or dangerous weapon license issued by this state on an electronic
12 device and shall give itthe license or digital image of the license to any activea law
13 enforcement officer for an inspection upon request by the officer. The failure of anyan
14 individual to give the license or digital image of the license to the officer is prima facie
15 evidence the individual is illegally carrying a firearm or dangerous weapon concealed.
16 2. EveryAn individual carrying a concealed firearm under the authority granted in
17 subsection 2 of section 62.1-04-02 shall inform a law enforcement officer of the
18 individual's possession of a concealed weapon upon the initiation of a traffic stop or
19 any other in-person contact initiated by a law enforcementfirearm if the officer inquires
20 about the individual possessing a concealed firearm.
21 3. EveryAn individual carrying a concealed firearm under the authority granted in
22 subsection 2 of section 62.1-04-02 shall have on one'sthe individual's person a valid
23 driver's license or nondriver identification card issued by the department of

1 transportation or by the individual's state or territory of residence, or a digital image of
2 one'sthe individual's valid driver's license or nondriver identification card on a mobile
3 device and shall provide the license or card to anya law enforcement officer for
4 inspection upon request by the officer.
5 4. An individual who violates this section is guilty of a noncriminal offense punishable by
6 a fee of twenty dollars.
