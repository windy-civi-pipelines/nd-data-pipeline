Section 1:
25.0698.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Warrey, Longmuir, Nelson, Novak, Ostlie, Rios
Senators Boschee, Cleary, Meyer, Sickler
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 40-49-12 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to cooperative purchasing contracts and cooperative purchasing for park
3 districts pursuant to a joint powers agreement.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new subsection to section 40-49-12 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 created and enacted as follows:
7 Participate in cooperative purchasing contracts with the office of management and
8 budget pursuant to chapter 54 - 44.4, participate in cooperative purchasing contracts
9 with another state, and contract for cooperative purchases pursuant to a joint powers
10 agreement under chapter 54 - 40.3 .
