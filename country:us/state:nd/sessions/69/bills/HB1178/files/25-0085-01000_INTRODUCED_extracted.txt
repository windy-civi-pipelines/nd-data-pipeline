Section 1:
25.0085.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Novak, Hager, Murphy, J. Olson, Schreiber-Beck, Swiontek
Senators Boehm, Cory, Mathern
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 15.1-07 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to school districts allowing students to leave campus to vote in elections.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 15.1-07 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 School districts - Policy - Voting.
7 Each school district shall adopt a policy allowing a student who is a qualified elector to
8 leave campus to vote in a general, special, or primary election. The policy may include
9 conditions for the student's absence, including a limitation on the length of time the student may
10 be absent or a designated time to vote.
