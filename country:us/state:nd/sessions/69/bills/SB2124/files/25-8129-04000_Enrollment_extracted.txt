Section 19:

specific company or person, including the name of any company or person who responded to
the data call.

SECTION 4. A new section to chapter 26.1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is created and

Market conduct annual statement.

Section 22:

market conduct annual statement. The statement must:

c. Be in the proper form and transmitted, as prescribed by the commissioner.

Section 23:
