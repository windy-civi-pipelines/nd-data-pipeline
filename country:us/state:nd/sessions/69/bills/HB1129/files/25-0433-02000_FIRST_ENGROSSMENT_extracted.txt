Section 1:
25.0433.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Richter, Mitskog, Murphy, O'Brien, Schauer
Senators Boschee, Meyer, Sickler
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the department of public instruction to address
2 student attendance and absenteeism.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTION - STUDENT
5 ATTENDANCE AND ABSENTEEISM - ONE-TIME FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any
6 moneys in the general fund in the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of
7 $1,000,000, or so much of the sum as may be necessary, to the department of public instruction
8 for the purpose of addressing student attendance and absenteeism in kindergarten through
9 grade twelve public schools, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30,
10 2027.
