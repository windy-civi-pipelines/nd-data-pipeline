25.3097.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly FIRST ENGROSSMENT
of North Dakota
ENGROSSED HOUSE
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 3023
Introduced by
Senator Boschee
1 A concurrent resolution affirming the 69th Legislative Assembly's commitment to ensuring
2 state-funded essential services remain fully funded and operational regardless of changes in
3 federal funding allocations.
4 WHEREAS, North Dakota citizens rely on a stable and functional government to provide
5 essential services, including K-12 education, law enforcement, public health, infrastructure,
6 agricultural programs, and economic development; and
7 WHEREAS, the potential for federal funding disruptions presents a risk to critical state
8 programs, affecting both urban and rural communities, including the state's farmers and
9 ranchers, along with the state's tribal nations; and
10 WHEREAS, the Legislative Assembly stands committed to supporting long-term solutions
11 that reduce dependency on federal uncertainty and reinforce state-level resilience in
12 governance and economic development; and
13 WHEREAS, the State of North Dakota has a duty to ensure continuity of government
14 operations and maintain essential services for the well-being and safety of all its residents;
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
16 NORTH DAKOTA, THE SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN:
17 That the Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly affirms its commitment to ensuring state-funded
18 essential services, including education, law enforcement, infrastructure, agricultural programs,
19 and public health remain fully funded and operational, regardless of changes in federal funding
20 allocations; and
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly urges the Governor
22 and state agencies to assess potential financial impacts of federal funding uncertainties and
23 identify solutions to mitigate disruptions to vital programs; and
Page No. 1 25.3097.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly urges the State of
2 North Dakota to engage with federal partners to advocate for the timely restoration of funding
3 that directly impacts the well-being of North Dakota citizens; and
4 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly urges the State of
5 North Dakota to foster continued collaboration with tribal nations to ensure shared governance
6 in maintaining essential services and economic stability for impacted communities; and
7 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Secretary of State forward copies of this resolution
8 to the Governor of North Dakota and each member of the North Dakota Congressional
9 Delegation.
