Section 1:
Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly of North Dakota
SENATE BILL NO. 2119
(State and Local Government Committee)
(North Dakota Public Employees Retirement System)
AN ACT to create and enact a new paragraph to subdivision h of subsection 3 of section 54-52-17 of
the North Dakota Century Code, relating to disability retirement benefit eligibility.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. A new paragraph to subdivision h of subsection 3 of section 54-52-17 of the North
Dakota Century Code is created and enacted as follows:
A member who has reached normal retirement age or normal retirement date and is
eligible for unreduced retirement benefits may not apply for a disability retirement
benefit.
