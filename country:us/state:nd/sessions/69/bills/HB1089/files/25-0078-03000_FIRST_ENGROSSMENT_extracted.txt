Section 1:
25.0078.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Hoverson, Maki, Nehring, Rios
Senator Magrum
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 1-08 of the North Dakota Century
2 Code, relating to regulations or plans adopted by international organizations.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 1-08 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
5 and enacted as follows:
6 Agendas, plans, or resolutions approved by an international organization ineffective
7 in this state.
8 An agenda, plan, or resolution adopted by the United Nations directly naming and imposing
9 a rule on this state or a political subdivision of this state which directly infringes upon or restricts
10 property rights of any resident of this state is void and ineffective. This section does not
11 preclude the legislative assembly or governing body of a political subdivision of this state from
12 enacting a law similar or identical to a United Nations agenda, plan, or resolution.
