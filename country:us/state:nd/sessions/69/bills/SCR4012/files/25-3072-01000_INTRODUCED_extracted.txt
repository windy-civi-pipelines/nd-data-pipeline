Section 1:
25.3072.01000
Sixty-ninth
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Klein, Erbele, Lemm
Representatives Beltz, Nelson
1 A concurrent resolution urging Congress to permit the use of M-44 sodium cyanide devices on
2 private land.
3 WHEREAS, M-44 sodium cyanide devices are effective tools used to control wild animals
4 that prey upon livestock, as well as threatened and endangered species; and
5 WHEREAS, the United States Department of Agriculture Wildlife Services uses the M-44
6 sodium cyanide devices to control predators and assist livestock producers; and
7 WHEREAS, when properly used, M-44 sodium cyanide devices pose no occupational risk
8 to humans; and
9 WHEREAS, United States Department of Agriculture Wildlife Services has employed strict
10 safety precautions including the use of warning signs and placing the devices only on private
11 land with written permission from the landowner; and
12 WHEREAS, the loss of the M-44 sodium cyanide device would impair the duties of the
13 United States Department of Agriculture Wildlife Services and would result in severe economic
14 consequences if predators remain unchecked;
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF NORTH DAKOTA, THE
16 HOUSE CONCURRING THEREIN:
17 That the Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly urges Congress to permit the use of M-44 sodium
18 cyanide devices on private land; and
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Secretary of State forward copies of this resolution
20 to the Majority and Minority Leaders of the United States Senate, the Majority and Minority
21 Leaders of the United States House of Representatives, and each member of the North Dakota
22 Congressional Delegation.
