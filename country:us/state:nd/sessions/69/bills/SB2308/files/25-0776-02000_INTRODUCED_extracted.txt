9 6-09-43. Health information technology planning loan fund - Appropriation.
10 1. The health information technology planning loan fund is established in the Bank for the
11 purpose of providing low-interest loans to health care entities to assist those entities in
12 improving health information technology infrastructure. This fund is a revolving loan
13 fund. All moneys transferred into the fund, interest upon moneys in the fund, and
14 collections of interest and principal on loans made from the fund are appropriated for
15 disbursement according to this section.
16 2. The Bank shall make loans from this fund to health care entities as approved by the
17 health information technology office director, in collaboration with the health
18 information technology advisory committee, in accordance with the criteria established
19 by the health information technology director under section 54-59-26.
20 3. The Bank shall administer the health information technology planning loan fund. Funds
21 in the loan fund may be used for loans as provided under this section and the costs of
22 administration of the fund. Annually, the Bank may deduct a service fee for
23 administering the revolving loan fund maintained under this section.
24 4. An application for a loan under this section must be made to the health information
25 technology office. The health information technology office director, in collaboration
26 with the health information technology advisory committee, may approve the
27 application of a qualified applicant that meets the criteria established by the health
28 information technology office director. The health information technology office shall
29 forward approved applications to the Bank. Upon approval of the application by the

2 this section.

5 collect interest and principal due the revolving loan fund under mortgages, contracts,
