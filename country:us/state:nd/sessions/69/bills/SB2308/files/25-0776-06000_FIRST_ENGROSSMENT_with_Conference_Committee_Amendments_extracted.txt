12 and reenacted as follows:
13 6-09-43. Health information technology planning loan fund - Appropriation.
14 1. The health information technology planning loan fund is established in the Bank for the
15 purpose of providing low-interest loans to health care entities to assist those entities in
16 improving health information technology infrastructure. This fund is a revolving loan
17 fund. All moneys transferred into the fund, interest upon moneys in the fund, and
18 collections of interest and principal on loans made from the fund are appropriated for
19 disbursement according to this section.
20 2. The Bank shall make loans from this fund to health care entities as approved by the
21 health information technology office director, in collaboration with the health
22 information technology advisory committee, in accordance with the criteria established
23 by the health information technology director under section 54-59-26.
24 3. The Bank shall administer the health information technology planning loan fund. Funds
25 in the loan fund may be used for loans as provided under this section and the costs of
26 administration of the fund. Annually, the Bank may deduct a service fee for
27 administering the revolving loan fund maintained under this section.
28 4. An application for a loan under this section must be made to the health information
29 technology office. The health information technology office director, in collaboration
30 with the health information technology advisory committee, may approve the

2 information technology office director. The health information technology office shall

5 this section.
6 5. The Bank may do all acts necessary to negotiate loans and preserve security as
