Section 1:
25.0776.04000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
SECOND ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Senators Roers, Hogue
Representatives Lefor, Louser
1 A BILL for an Act to provide for a legislative management study regarding the duties and
2 responsibilities of boards.
3 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - REVIEW AND ASSESSMENT OF
4 BOARDS. During the 2025-26 interim, the legislative management shall consider studying the
5 duties and responsibilities of all boards. The study must include a review and assessment of all
6 boards for inefficiencies and duplication of responsibilities, and consider how government
7 operations can be minimized and streamlined through the consolidation or elimination of
8 boards. The legislative management shall report its findings and recommendations, together
9 with any legislation necessary to implement the recommendations, to the seventieth legislative
10 assembly.
