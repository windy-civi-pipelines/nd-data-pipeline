Section 1:
25.0630.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Beltz, Dobervich, Hagert, Kiefert, Nehring, Tveit
Senators Hogan, Lemm, Weber
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 5 of section 4.1-07-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the definitions applying to the dry pea and lentil council.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 5 of section 4.1-07-01 of the North Dakota Century
5 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 5. "Producer" means any person that:
7 a. Plants or causes to be planted a dry pea and lentil crop in which the person has
8 an ownership interest, with the intent that upon maturity the crop will be
9 harvested; or
10 b. Will have met the requirements of subdivision a during the next available growing
11 season; or
12 c. Has met the requirements of subdivision a during the immediately preceding
13 growing seasonplanted and subsequently will plant a dry pea and lentil crop as
14 part of the person's crop rotation within a six - year period .
