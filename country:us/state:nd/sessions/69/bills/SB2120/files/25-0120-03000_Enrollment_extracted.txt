Section 1:
Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly of North Dakota
SENATE BILL NO. 2120
(State and Local Government Committee)
(North Dakota Public Employees Retirement System)
AN ACT to provide for a transfer to the highway patrol troopers' retirement system fund.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. TRANSFER - STRATEGIC INVESTMENT AND IMPROVEMENTS FUND TO
HIGHWAY PATROL TROOPERS' RETIREMENT SYSTEM FUND. The office of management and
budget shall transfer the sum of $15,000,000 from the strategic investment and improvements fund to
the highway patrol troopers' retirement system fund, for the purpose of increasing the funded liability of
the highway patrol troopers' retirement plan during the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending
June 30, 2027.

(North Dakota Public Employees Retirement System)

the highway patrol troopers' retirement plan during the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending
