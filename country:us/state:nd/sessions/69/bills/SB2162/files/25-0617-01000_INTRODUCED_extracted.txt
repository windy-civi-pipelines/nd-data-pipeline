Section 1:
25.0617.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Kessel, Thomas, Weber
Representatives Hagert, Kempenich
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 4.1-13-15 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to the assessment levied by the wheat commission.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 4.1-13-15 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 4.1-13-15. Assessment.
7 An assessment at the rate of fifteenthirty mills per bushel [35.24 liters] by weight is imposed
8 upon all wheat:
9 1. All wheat grownGrown in this state, at the time of its sale;
10 2. All wheat deliveredDelivered into this state, at the time of its sale; and
11 3. All wheat soldSold through commercial channels to a first purchaser in this state.
