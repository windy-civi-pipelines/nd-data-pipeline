Section 1:
25.1228.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Senators Roers, Hogan, Larson
Representatives Heinert, O'Brien, Schneider
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 14-07 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the disclosure of domestic violence protection orders to law
3 enforcement.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 14-07 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Disclosure of court records to law enforcement.
8 A court record maintained in relation to a matter involving a domestic violence protection
9 order under section 14 - 07.1 - 02 must be disclosed and provided upon request to the office of a
10 law enforcement officer to enable the law enforcement officer to carry out the duties of the law
11 enforcement officer.
