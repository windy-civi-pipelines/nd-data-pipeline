Section 1:
25.8114.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Agriculture Committee
(At the request of the Agriculture Commissioner)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 36-25-01 of North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to the definition of farmed elk.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 36-25-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 36-25-01. Definitions.
7 In this chapter, unless the context otherwise requires:
8 1. "Board" means the state board of animal health.
9 2. "Commissioner" means the agriculture commissioner.
10 3. "Farmed elk" means mammals of the elk family (cervus elaphus), except red
11 deer,North American elk genus and species (cervus canadensis) confined in a
12 manmade enclosure designed to prevent escape and:
13 a. Raised for fiber, meat, or animal byproducts;
14 b. Raised for breeding, exhibition, or harvest; or
15 c. Maintained for any other purpose.
16 4. "Owner" means a person who owns or is responsible for the raising of farmed elk.
