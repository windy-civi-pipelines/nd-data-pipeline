Section 1:
25.8039.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Transportation Committee
(At the request of the Department of Transportation)
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 3 of section 24-02-37 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the special road fund.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 3 of section 24-02-37 of the North Dakota Century
5 Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 3. The state treasurer shall deposit the moneys in the state highway fund in an
7 interest-bearing account at the Bank of North Dakota. The state treasurer shall deposit
8 eighty percent of the income derived from the interest-bearing account in a special
9 interest-bearing account in the state treasury known as the special road fund. The
10 special road fund may be used, within the limits of legislative appropriation, exclusively
11 for the construction and maintenance of access roads to and roads within recreational,
12 tourist, and historical areas as designated by the special road committee. A political
13 subdivision, tribal government, or state agency may request funds from the special
14 road fund by applying to the committee on forms designated by the committee. The
15 committee may require the political subdivision, tribal government, or state agency to
16 contribute to the cost of the project as a condition of any expenditure authorized from
17 the special road fund. Any moneys in the fund not obligated by the special road
18 committee by June thirtieth of each odd-numbered year must be held for an additional
19 two years after which the funds revert to the state highway fund.
