Section 1:
25.0846.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Boehm, Castaneda, Wobbema
Representatives Frelich, Henderson, Holle
1 A BILL for an Act to provide a legislative management study relating to the retention of library
2 materials.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY - RETENTION OF LIBRARY
5 MATERIALS. During the 2025-26 interim, the legislative management shall consider studying
6 the feasibility and desirability of requiring libraries maintained by state agencies, including the
7 state library and libraries at institutions under the control of the state board of higher education,
8 to retain a copy of any book or other written material that contains the material's original text if
9 the publisher of the material publishes a new version to update language that was common
10 when the the material was originally published to language that is considered more socially
11 progressive, politically correct, inclusive, or less socially offensive. The study must include an
12 analysis of how artificial intelligence has or will affect the retention of library materials and how
13 library materials are updated or altered using artificial intelligence. The legislative management
14 shall report its findings and recommendations, together with any legislation required to
15 implement the recommendations, to the seventieth legislative assembly.
