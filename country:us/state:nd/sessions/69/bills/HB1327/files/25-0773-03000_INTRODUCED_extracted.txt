Section 1:
25.0773.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Hendrix, Christianson, Koppelman, Maki, D. Ruby, Schatz
Senators Clemens, Walen
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 39-04-10 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to special plates for amateur radio station licenseholders.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 39-04-10 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 39-04-10. Special plates for amateur radio station licenseholders.
7 Owners of motor vehicles required to be registered under subdivisions a and b of
8 subsection 2 of section 39-04-19, who hold an unrevoked and unexpired official amateur radio
9 station license issued by the federal communications commission, Washington, D.C., may
10 receive special plates. The plates will be issued upon application to the department,
11 accompanied by proof of ownership of the amateur radio station license, compliance with the
12 state motor vehicle laws relating to registration and licensing of motor vehicles, and payment of
13 the regular license fee, as prescribed under the North Dakota motor vehicle laws. The special
14 plates will be issued in lieu of the plates ordinarily issued, and must have inscribed on themthe
15 plates the official amateur radio call letters of the applicant as assigned by the federal
16 communications commission and the the words "AMATEUR RADIO". Applicants under this
17 section may apply for up to five plates with the same call letters. The department shall add an
18 additional ascending number from two to five to each subsequent plate issued to the same
19 applicant under this section.
