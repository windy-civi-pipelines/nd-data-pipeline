25 civil penalty not to exceed twenty-five thousand dollars.

16 and enacted as follows:
17 Prohibited ownership near military installations - Penalty .
18 1. For purposes of this section:
19 a. "Critical infrastructure" has the meaning provided in section 44 - 04 - 24 .
20 b. "Foreign country of concern" has the meaning provided in section 47 - 01 - 09 .
21 c. "Foreign organization of concern" has the meaning provided in section 47 - 01 - 09 .
22 d. "Military installation" has the meaning provided in section 15.1 - 04.1 - 01 .
23 2. Notwithstanding section 47 - 01 - 09 or chapter 47 - 10.1, a foreign country of concern or
24 foreign organization of concern may not:
25 a. Lease, purchase, or otherwise acquire title to critical infrastructure or real
26 property within ten miles [16.09 kilometers] of a military installation located within
27 this state; or
28 b. Enter a contract with a person under which a foreign country of concern or
29 foreign organization of concern has direct or indirect control of critical
30 infrastructure located within this state.
