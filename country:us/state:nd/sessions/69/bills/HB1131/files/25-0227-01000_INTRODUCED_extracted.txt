Section 1:
25.0227.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Holle, VanWinkle, Conmy, Fisher, Hauck, Hendrix, Toman, J. Olson
Senators Myrdal, Schaible, Van Oosting, Weston
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 4.1-25-40.1 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to the sale of raw milk products; and to provide a penalty.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 4.1-25-40.1 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 4.1-25-40.1. Sale of raw milk or raw milk products directly to a consumer -
7 Prohibitions - Exemptions.
8 1. A farm may sell raw milk or raw milk products directly to the end consumer in this state
9 for personal consumption.
10 2. A farm may not sell raw milk or raw milk products to a wholesaler or retail store for
11 mass consumption under this chapter. The seller shall onlymay not sell raw milk
12 withinor raw milk products outside this state. The sale may not involve interstate
13 commerce. Raw milk or raw milk products may not be donated.
14 3. A farm selling raw milk or raw milk products under the provisions of this section is not
15 subject to any other provision of this chapter, chapters 4.1-05, 4.1-26, 19-02.1, or
16 23-9, or title 64.
