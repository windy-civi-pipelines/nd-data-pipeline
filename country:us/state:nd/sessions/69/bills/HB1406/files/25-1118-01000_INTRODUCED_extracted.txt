Section 1:
25.1118.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives D. Johnston, Christianson, Hendrix, Kasper, Koppelman, Rohr, Toman,
VanWinkle
Senators Magrum, Paulson
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 54-03 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the introduction of bills prepared by executive branch agencies and
3 the judicial branch.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 54-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Introduction of bills prepared by executive branch agencies and the judicial branch.
8 Executive branch agencies and the judicial branch may not submit a legislative bill for
9 introduction to the legislative assembly until the first legislative filing deadline for legislators. The
10 legislative council shall provide notice of the deadline to executive branch agencies and the
11 judicial branch in December of each even-numbered year.
