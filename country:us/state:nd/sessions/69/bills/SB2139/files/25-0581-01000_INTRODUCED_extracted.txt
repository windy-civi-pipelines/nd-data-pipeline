Section 1:
25.0581.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Patten, Erbele, Wanzek
Representatives Brandenburg, Kempenich
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact section 4.1-73-14 of the North Dakota Century Code,
2 relating to the fees for recording and rerecording livestock brands.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 4.1-73-14 of the North Dakota Century Code is
5 amended and reenacted as follows:
6 4.1-73-14. Recording and rerecording brands - Fee.
7 Each application for recording or rerecording a brand must be accompanied by a fee in the
8 amount of twenty-fivefifty dollars.
