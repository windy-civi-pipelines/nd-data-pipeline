Section 1:
25.0962.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Louser, Ista, Lefor
Senators Hogan, Hogue
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 54-03 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to access to requests to the legislative council made by members of the
3 legislative assembly.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 54-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Requests for legislation - Legislative council - Disclosure to caucus leader.
8 1. The legislative council shall disclose to the caucus leader of the requester's political
9 faction the topic of a request to the legislative council made by a member of the
10 legislative assembly for a draft bill or resolution or a revision to a draft bill or resolution
11 and the name of the requester.
12 2. A draft or revision of a draft bill or resolution drafted for a member of the legislative
13 assembly may be disclosed to the caucus leader of the requester's political faction,
14 unless the member of the legislative assembly elects to maintain the confidentiality of
15 the draft or revision of a draft bill or resolution with respect to the caucus leader of the
16 requester's political faction in the manner prescribed by the legislative council.
17 3. Records disclosed to the caucus leader of the requester's political faction under this
18 section are not subject to section 44 - 04 - 18 and section 6 of article XI of the
19 Constitution of North Dakota.
