25.3108.01000
Sixty-ninth
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Memorial Resolutions Committee
1 A memorial resolution for deceased members of the Senate of North Dakota.
2 WHEREAS, God has welcomed to their eternal home our former colleagues:
3 Dwight Cook, who served in the 55th through the 66th Legislative Assemblies, from
4 District 34, died January 7, 2024;
5 Judy Lee DeMers, who served in the 48th through the 52nd Legislative Assemblies, from
6 District 17-18, and in the 53rd through the 56th Legislative Assemblies, from District 18, died
7 March 12, 2024;
8 Richard Wayne Goldberg, who served in the 40th through the 43rd Legislative Assemblies,
9 from District 21, died July 18, 2023;
10 Daniel Fred Jerome, who served in the 52nd and 53rd Legislative Assemblies, from District 9,
11 died May 25, 2024;
12 Douglas Allen Larsen, who served in the 67th and 68th Legislative Assemblies, from
13 District 34, died October 1, 2023;
14 Donald B. Moore, who served in the 42nd Legislative Assembly and in the 47th through the
15 52nd Legislative Assemblies, from District 28, died November 30, 2023;
16 David E. "Dave" Nething, who served in the 40th through the 44th Legislative Assemblies,
17 from District 29, in the 45th through the 57th Legislative Assemblies, from District 48, and in the
18 58th through the 62nd Legislative Assemblies, from District 12, died December 2, 2024;
19 Randy A. Schobinger, who served in the 54th through the 59th Legislative Assemblies, from
20 District 3, and in the 65th through the 68th Legislative Assemblies, from District 40, died
21 August 13, 2024;
22 Herbert A. "Herb" Urlacher, who served in the 51st and 52nd Legislative Assemblies, from
23 District 38, and in the 53rd through the 60th Legislative Assemblies, from District 36, died
24 October 29, 2023; and
Page No. 1 25.3108.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 WHEREAS, we now pause to mourn the passing of our former Senate colleagues and to
2 honor their memories; and
3 WHEREAS, these legislators rendered outstanding service to the people of the state by
4 their contributions to public service;
5 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 That we express our sorrow on their passing and our appreciation, on behalf of the people
7 of North Dakota, of the loyal and devoted service of our former colleagues; and
8 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that for the perpetuation of their memory this token of
9 respect and sympathy by their successors in trust be printed in the Journal of the Senate and
10 that the Secretary of State present enrolled copies of this resolution to the surviving families of
11 these deceased Senators.
