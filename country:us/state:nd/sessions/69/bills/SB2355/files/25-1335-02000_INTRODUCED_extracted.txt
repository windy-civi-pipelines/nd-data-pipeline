Section 1:
25.1335.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Dwyer, Beard, Hogue
Representatives Schreiber-Beck, Lefor, Rohr
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 15.1-21 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to intelligent design in science content standards for elementary and
3 secondary students.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 15.1-21 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Intelligent design - Required science content standard.
8 The superintendent of public instruction shall include intelligent design in the state science
9 content standards for elementary, middle, and high school students by Augus t 1, 2027. The
10 superintendent shall provide teachers with instructional materials demonstrating intelligent
11 design is a viable scientific theory for the creation of all life forms and provide in - service training
12 necessary to include intelligent design as part of the science content standards.
