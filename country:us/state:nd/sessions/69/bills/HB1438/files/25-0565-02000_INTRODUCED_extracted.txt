Section 1:
25.0565.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Hanson, Foss
Senator Boschee
1 A BILL for an Act to provide an appropriation to the state historical society for a historic theater
2 restoration grant program.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION - STATE HISTORICAL SOCIETY - HISTORIC THEATER
5 RESTORATION GRANT PROGRAM - MATCHING FUNDS REQUIREMENT - ONE-TIME
6 FUNDING. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the general fund in the state treasury,
7 not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $1,100,000, or so much of the sum as may be
8 necessary, to the state historical society for the purpose of providing a grant to an organization
9 dedicated to the restoration of a historic theater in a city with a population of more than 100,000,
10 for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2027. The state historical society
11 may spend the funds identified in this section only to the extent the organization has secured
12 matching funds from nonstate sources on a dollar-for-dollar basis. This funding is considered a
13 one-time funding item.
