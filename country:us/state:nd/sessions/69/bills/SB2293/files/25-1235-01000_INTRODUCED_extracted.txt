Section 1:
25.1235.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Roers, Cleary, Meyer
Representatives Dockter, M. Ruby, Vetter
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsections 7 and 18 of section 19-24.1-01, and
2 subdivision c of subsection 2 of section 19-24.1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating
3 to medical marijuana container sizes, caregivers, and documentation.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsections 7 and 18 of section 19-24.1-01 of the North
6 Dakota Century Code are amended and reenacted as follows:
7 7. "Cannabinoid concentrate" means a concentrate or extract obtained by separating
8 cannabinoids from marijuana by a mechanical, chemical, or other process. A container
9 holding a cannabinoid concentrate for dispensing may not exceed one gram .
10 18. "Designated caregiver" means an individual who is at least twenty - one years of age
11 and agrees to manage the well-being of a registered qualifying patient with respect to
12 the qualifying patient's medical use of marijuana.
13 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Subdivision c of subsection 2 of section 19-24.1-03 of the
14 North Dakota Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
15 c. An original qualifying patient application for a registry identification card form
16 established by the department which must include all of the following:
17 (1) The applicant's name, address, and date of birth.
18 (2) The name, address, and date of birth of the applicant's proposed
19 designated caregiver, if any.
20 (3) A photographic copy of the applicant's North Dakota identification. The
21 North Dakota identification must be available for inspection and verification
22 upon request of the department. If the applicant is unable to obtain a current
23 North Dakota identification due to the applicant's medical condition, the
24 applicant may submit other department approved documentation. If the
Page No. 1 25.1235.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 applicant is a minor, a certified copy of a birth record or a photographic copy
2 of the minor's North Dakota identification is required.
3 (4) The applicant's or guardian's signature and the date, or in the case of a
4 minor, the signature of the minor's parent or legal guardian with
5 responsibility for health care decisions and the date.
6 (5) A disclosure that possession of a firearm by a person who possesses
7 marijuana may be a violation of federal law.
