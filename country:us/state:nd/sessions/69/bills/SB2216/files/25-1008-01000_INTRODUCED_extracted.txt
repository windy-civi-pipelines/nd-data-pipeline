Section 1:
25.1008.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senator Cleary
Representatives Bosch, Porter
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 20.1-02 and a new section to
2 chapter 20.1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to waterfowl habitat restoration
3 stamp fees and the waterfowl habitat improvement fund.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 20.1-02 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 Waterfowl habitat improvement fund.
8 There is created in the state treasury a special fund known as the waterfowl habitat
9 improvement fund. The fund consists of transfers and deposits made in accordance with
10 section 2 of this Act and interest earned on moneys in the fund. Moneys in the fund may be
11 spent, subject to legislative appropriations, for improving or restoring waterfowl habitat and
12 supporting youth hunting programs.
13 SECTION 2. A new section to chapter 20.1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
14 and enacted as follows:
15 Waterfowl habitat restoration stamp required - Use of revenue.
16 A waterfowl habitat restoration stamp is required for every resident small game license for
17 which a stamp fee of ten dollars must be charged. The waterfowl habitat restoration stamp fee
18 is in addition to the annual small game license fee charged under section 20.1 - 03 - 12. All money
19 generated by waterfowl habitat restoration stamp fees must be placed in the waterfowl habitat
20 improvement fund.
