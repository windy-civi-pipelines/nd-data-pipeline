Section 1:
25.1164.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Jonas, Murphy, Richter, Sanford, Hager
Senators Bekkedahl, Boschee
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 15-10 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to the creation of the North Dakota student teacher scholarship program;
3 and to provide an appropriation.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 15-10 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
6 and enacted as follows:
7 North Dakota student teacher scholarship program.
8 1. The state board of higher education shall:
9 a. Develop and administer the North Dakota student teacher scholarship program to
10 provide scholarships for student teachers who will seek initial teacher licensure
11 after graduation;
12 b. Distribute funds for the scholarships to each institution of higher education in the
13 state offering a state-approved teacher preparation program; and
14 c. Provide guidance and assistance to an institution of higher education to
15 administer the program.
16 2. To be eligible to receive a scholarship under this section, a student must:
17 a. Be enrolled in a state-approved teacher preparation program and be completing
18 the student teaching experience in the state;
19 b. Be progressing satisfactorily through the teacher preparation program, as
20 determined by the institution of higher education; and
21 c. Not hold a teaching license.
22 3. An institution of higher education shall apply a scholarship not exceeding three
23 thousand dollars to an eligible student's tuition and fees for the semester the student is
24 completing the student teaching experience.
Page No. 1 25.1164.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 SECTION 2. APPROPRIATION - STATE BOARD OF HIGHER EDUCATION - STUDENT
2 TEACHER SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM. There is appropriated out of any moneys in the
3 general fund in the state treasury, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $4,200,000, or so
4 much of the sum as may be necessary, to the state board of higher education for the purpose of
5 providing funds to institutions of higher education to award scholarships to students who are
6 eligible to receive a student teacher scholarship, for the biennium beginning July 1, 2025, and
7 ending June 30, 2027.
