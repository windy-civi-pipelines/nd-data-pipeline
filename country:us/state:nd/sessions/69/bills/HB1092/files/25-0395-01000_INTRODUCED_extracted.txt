Section 1:
25.0395.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Vigesaa, Headland
Senator Wanzek
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota
2 Century Code, relating to designating the overpass on United States highway 52 as the S/Sgt.
3 Miles Shelley WWII overpass; to provide a continuing appropriation; and to declare an
4 emergency.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. A new section to chapter 24-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is created
7 and enacted as follows:
8 S/Sgt. Miles Shelley WWII overpass - United States highway 52 - Continuing
9 appropriation.
10 The department shall designate the overpass on United States highway 52 over the railway
11 in Carrington between the junction of state highway 200 and the junction of second street north
12 as the S/Sgt. Miles Shelley WWII bridge and shall place signs along the highway designating
13 that name. The department may accept any appropriate signs or funds donated to the
14 department for the placement of the signs. Any donated funds are appropriated to the
15 department on a continuing basis for the purpose of providing signs designating the overpass
16 on United States highway 52 as the S/Sgt. Miles Shelley WWII bridge.
17 SECTION 2. EMERGENCY. This Act is declared to be an emergency measure.
