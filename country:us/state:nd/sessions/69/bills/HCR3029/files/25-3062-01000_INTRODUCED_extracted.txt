Section 1:
25.3062.01000
Sixty-ninth
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Dobervich, Ista, Davis
Senators Braunberger, Boschee
1 A concurrent resolution directing the Legislative Management to consider studying inpatient
2 services, community-based services, and existing waivers for treatment and support of
3 individuals with neurological conditions and behavioral symptoms that put the individual at risk
4 for incarceration, inappropriate placement, or homelessness.
5 WHEREAS, the Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly recognizes that behavioral symptoms of
6 neurological conditions often result in incarceration, inappropriate placement in geriatric
7 psychiatry care facilities, long-term placement at the state hospital, and high rates of
8 homelessness; and
9 WHEREAS, the Sixty-ninth Legislative Assembly recognizes the value in collecting and
10 analyzing data regarding existing inpatient services, community-based services, and waivers for
11 treatment and support of individuals with neurological conditions;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
13 NORTH DAKOTA, THE SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN:
14 That the Legislative Management consider studying inpatient services, community-based
15 services, and existing waivers for treatment and support of individuals with neurological
16 conditions and behavioral symptoms that put the individual at risk for incarceration,
17 inappropriate placement, or homelessness; and
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the study include an analysis of available data,
19 identification of gaps in services, a review of appropriate treatment and support services, and
20 recommendations for a waiver program, new level of care, and appropriate placement
21 guidelines, to fund and deliver services to meet the care and support needs of individuals living
22 with a neurological condition; and
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Legislative Management report its findings and
24 recommendations, together with any legislation required to implement the recommendations, to
25 the Seventieth Legislative Assembly.
