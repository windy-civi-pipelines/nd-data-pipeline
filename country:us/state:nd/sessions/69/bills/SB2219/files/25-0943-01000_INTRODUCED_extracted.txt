Section 1:
25.0943.01000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Senators Cleary, Rummel, Weber
Representatives Hanson, Mitskog, Murphy
1 A BILL for an Act to create and enact a new subsection to section 54-24-03 and a new
2 subsection to section 54-24.2-01 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to disbursement of
3 surplus library materials and the eligibility of public libraries to receive financial aid.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. A new subsection to section 54-24-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 created and enacted as follows:
7 Disburse surplus library materials to libraries throughout the state.
8 SECTION 2. A new subsection to section 54-24.2-01 of the North Dakota Century Code is
9 created and enacted as follows:
10 Complete and submit the annual report required under section 54 - 24.2 - 06 for the
11 previous calendar year.
