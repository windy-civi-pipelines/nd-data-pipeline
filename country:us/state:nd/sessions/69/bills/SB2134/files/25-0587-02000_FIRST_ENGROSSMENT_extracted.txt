Section 1:
25.0587.02000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Senators Roers, Davison, Dever
Representatives Dockter, Schauer, Stemen
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 1 of section 15-39.1-05.1 of the North
2 Dakota Century Code, relating to the composition of the teachers' fund for retirement board.
3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
4 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 1 of section 15-39.1-05.1 of the North Dakota
5 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
6 1. The authority to set policy for the fund rests in a board of trustees composed as
7 follows:
8 a. The governor shall appoint, from a list of three nominees submitted to the
9 governor by North Dakota united, two board members who are actively employed
10 as elementary or secondary teachers in full-time positions not classified as
11 school administrators. A board member appointed under this subdivision who
12 terminates employment may not continue to serve as a member of the board.
13 b. The governor shall appoint, from a list of three nominees submitted to the
14 governor by the North Dakota council of educational leaders, one board member
15 who is actively employed as a full-time school administrator. A board member
16 appointed under this subdivision who terminates employment may not continue
17 to serve as a member of the board.
18 c. The governor shall appoint, from a list of three nominees submitted to the
19 governor by the North Dakota retired teachers association, two board members
20 who are the retired members of the fund.

1 d. The state treasurer, or a designee from the office of state treasurer, and the
2 superintendent of public instruction, or a designee from the department of public
3 instruction.
