Section 1:
25.3102.01000
Sixty-ninth
of North Dakota
Introduced by
Representatives Vetter, Dockter, Dressler, Grueneich, Hendrix, D. Johnston, Christianson,
Bahl
Senators Castaneda, Barta
1 A concurrent resolution directing the Legislative Management to consider studying personal
2 legislative staff for the Legislative Assembly.
3 WHEREAS, in anticipation of the legislative turnover that will come with legislative term
4 limits, it is vital to equip legislators and future legislators with all necessary support to carry out
5 responsibilities effectively and efficiently; and
6 WHEREAS, the option to hire personal legislative staff would provide legislators the
7 opportunity to better plan, prioritize, and delegate, which would increase productivity to best
8 serve citizens of this state;
9 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF
10 NORTH DAKOTA, THE SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN:
11 That the Legislative Management consider studying the impact and benefits of personal
12 legislative staff for the Legislative Assembly, including a review of other states that allow
13 legislators to hire personal staff; and
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Legislative Management report its findings and
15 recommendations, together with any legislation required to implement the recommendations, to
16 the Seventieth Legislative Assembly.
