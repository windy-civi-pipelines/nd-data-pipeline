17 amended and reenacted as follows:
18 12.1-04.1-01. Standard for lack of criminal responsibility.
19 1. An individual is not criminally responsible for criminal conduct if, as a result of mental
20 disease or defect existing at the time the conduct occurs:
21 a. The individual lacks substantial capacity to comprehend the harmful nature or
22 consequences of the conduct, or the conduct is the result of a loss or serious
23 distortion of the individual's capacity to recognize reality; and
24 b. It is an essential element of the crime charged that the individual act willfully.
