Section 1:
25.1079.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
of North Dakota
FIRST ENGROSSMENT
Introduced by
Representatives Bosch, Lefor
Senator Hogue
1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact sections 16.1-09-02, 16.1-09-03, and 16.1-09-05 of the
2 North Dakota Century Code, relating to the statement of interests filed with the secretary of
3 state.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 16.1-09-02 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 amended and reenacted as follows:
7 16.1-09-02. Statement of interests to be filed.
8 1. Every candidate for elective office shall sign and file the statement of interests as
9 required by this chapter. In a year when a president and vice president of the United
10 States are to be chosen, presidential and vice presidential candidates shall file with
11 the secretary of state either a statement of interests as required by this chapter or a
12 copy of the personal disclosure statement that is required by the federal election
13 commission. Candidates for elective office who are required to file suchthe statements
14 shall do sofile the statements with the filing officer for that election at the time of filing a
15 certificate of nomination, a certificate of endorsement, a petition of nomination, or a
16 certificate of write-in candidacy, pursuant to chapter 16.1-11, 16.1-12, or 40-21, as is
17 appropriate.
18 2. An individual who has filed a statement as the result of candidacy in a primary election
19 need not refile before running in the following general election. A write-in candidate
20 who is not required to file a certificate of write-in candidacy shall file the statement of
21 interests after the candidate's election at the time of filing the required oath of office.
22 Every individual who is appointed by the governor to a state agency, board, bureau,
Page No. 1 25.1079.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 commission, department, or occupational or professional licensing board shall file a
2 statement of interests as required by this chapter with the secretary of state
3 simultaneously with announcement of the appointment.
4 3. A filing officer may not include a candidate's name on the ballot if an error is
5 discovered on the statement and the candidate is unable to or refuses to make the
6 necessary correction before the sixty-fourth day before the election.
7 4. While holding office, an individual required to file the statement of interests under this
8 section, except candidates elected to hold federal office, shall submit an annual
9 statement of interests to the appropriate filing officer by January thirty - first. A filing
10 officer shall collect fees for late filings pursuant to section 16.1 - 08.1 - 06.1 .
11 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Section 16.1-09-03 of the North Dakota Century Code is
12 amended and reenacted as follows:
13 16.1-09-03. Contents of statement of interests.
14 The statement of interests required to be filed under this chapter applies to the candidate or
15 appointee and that person's spouse and must include:
16 1. The name of the business or employer and an identification of the principal source of
17 income, defined in the state income tax return as "principal occupation", of both the
18 candidate or appointee and that person's spouse.
19 2. The name of each business or trust, not the principal source of income, in which the
20 person making the statement, and that person's spouse, have a financial interest.
21 3. A list of the associations or institutions with which the person making the statement,
22 and that person's spouse, are closely associated, or for which they serve as a director
23 or officer, and which may be affected by legislative action, in the case of a statement
24 submitted by a legislative candidate, or action by the candidate or appointee in that
25 person's capacity as an officeholder.
26 4. The identity by name of all business offices, business directorships, and fiduciary
27 relationships the person making the statement, and that person's spouse, have held in
28 the preceding calendar year.
29 5. A list of political subdivisions and any agency or department of the state to which the
30 person providing the statement, or the person's spouse, sold goods or services of
31 more than five thousand dollars in the preceding calendar year from:
Page No. 2 25.1079.03000
Sixty-ninth
Legislative Assembly
1 a. An employer identified under subsection 1 in which the person or the person's
2 spouse has an ownership interest of ten percent or more; or
3 b. A business or trust identified in subsection 2 in which the person or the person's
4 spouse has an ownership interest of ten percent or more.
5 SECTION 3. AMENDMENT. Section 16.1-09-05 of the North Dakota Century Code is
6 amended and reenacted as follows:
7 16.1-09-05. Powers and duties of the secretary of state and county and city auditors.
8 The secretary of state, or the county or city auditor, when appropriate shall:
9 1. Accept and file any statement submitted pursuant to this chapter.
10 2. Make statements filed available for public inspection and copying during regular office
11 hours. A reasonable fee may be charged to cover the cost of copying. Proceeds from
12 any fees charged must be deposited in the general fund of the appropriate
13 governmental entity.
14 3. Publish all statements filed with the secretary of state under this chapter on a website
15 maintained by the secretary of state.
16 4. Preserve statements filed under this chapter for the term of office to which the person
17 making disclosure is elected or appointed or until a new statement is filed and
18 preserve. Preserve statements filed pursuant tounder this chapter by those candidates
19 who are not elected or appointed for a period of one year after the date of receipt.
